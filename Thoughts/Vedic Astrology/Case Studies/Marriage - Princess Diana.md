## Foundation Analysis: D-1 Rasi Chart

### Lagna Analysis (Sagittarius)
Sagittarius rising gives a tall, athletic frame and a naturally friendly, open, and somewhat naive personality.
-   **Lagna Lord Jupiter:** Placed in **2nd House** (Capricorn).
-   **Condition:** Jupiter is **Debilitated** (Neecha).
-   **Retrograde:** A retrograde debilitated planet acts somewhat strong (*Ucchabhilashi*), but the foundational weakness remains.
-   **Implication:** The "Self" (Jupiter) goes into the "Royal Family" (2nd House/Capricorn) but feels weak/debilitated there. She felt crushed by the protocols of the monarchy.

### The 7th House (Marriage) Analysis - A Textbook Affliction

The 7th house governs the spouse and marital happiness. In Diana's chart, this area is severely under siege:

1.  **Sun in 7th House:**
    -   The Sun is a **Krura Graha** (Cruel Planet) by nature. In the 7th, it burns up marital bliss (*Dampatya Sukha*).
    -   It creates an ego battle. The spouse (Charles) is "Solar" (Heir to the throne, authoritative), leaving Diana in the shadow.

2.  **Mercury in 7th House:**
    -   Mercury is the **7th Lord** (Spouse).
    -   It is **Retrograde** (Unstable).
    -   It is **Combust** (Burnt by the Sun).
    -   **Result:** The spouse (7th Lord) is dominant (Sun) but emotionally unavailable or inconsistent (Retrograde). Communication (Mercury) in the marriage is "burnt out".

3.  **Ketu in 3rd / Rahu in 9th (Aspecting 7th):**
    -   Wait, Rahu in Leo (9th) does not aspect Gemini (7th) by standard aspect.
    -   However, **Saturn (2nd Lord)** in Capricorn casts its **10th aspect** onto the 7th House (Libra? No, Capricorn -> Aquarius, Pisces, Aries, Taurus, Gemini? No. 10th aspect from Cap reaches Libra (11th house). 7th aspect reaches Cancer. 3rd aspect reaches Pisces).
    -   Correction: **Saturn in Capricorn aspecting...**
        -   3rd Aspect: Pisces (4th House - Home).
        -   7th Aspect: Cancer (8th House - Scandal).
        -   10th Aspect: Libra (11th House).
    -   **Mars (in Leo) aspects:**
        -   4th Aspect: Scorpio (12th House).
        -   7th Aspect: Aquarius (3rd House).
        -   8th Aspect: Pisces (4th House).

4.  **Papakartari Yoga on 7th House:**
    -   The 7th House (Gemini) is hemmed in?
    -   6th House has Venus.
    -   8th House has... (Empty).
    -   Not a strict Papakartari.

### Venus Analysis (Karaka for Love)

**Venus** is the natural significator of marriage.
-   **Placement:** **6th House** (Taurus).
-   **Significance:** Venus in the 6th is **Marana Karaka Sthana** (Death-like placement for significations). It turns "Love" into "Conflict/Service".
-   **Result:** Her search for love led to enemies, service to the sick (AIDS/Leprosy work - positive 6th house), and eventual divorce.

### Mars-Rahu Conjunction (Angaraka Yoga)
-   **Mars and Rahu** are conjunct in **Leo (9th House)**.
-   **Effect:** Rebellion against tradition (9th House). Explosive energy.
-   Mars aspects the **4th House** (Domestic Peace) with its 8th aspect. This destroyed her peace of mind and home life.

## Dasha Analysis (Timing of Events)

### Marriage (July 29, 1981)
**Dasha:** **Rahu Mahadasha / Jupiter Antardasha** (Rahu-Guru).
-   **Rahu:** In 9th (House of Fortune/Ceremony). Conjunct Mars (5th Lord - Romance).
-   **Jupiter:** Lagna Lord (Self) in 2nd (Family).
-   **Event:** A massive royal wedding. Rahu brought the foreign/royal destiny; Jupiter brought the family status. But Rahu-Jupiter is often a "Guru-Chandala" like energy—expansion based on illusion.

### Divorce (August 1996)
**Dasha:** **Jupiter Mahadasha / Venus Antardasha** (Guru-Shukra).
-   **Jupiter:** Debilitated Lagna Lord.
-   **Venus:** 6th Lord (Divorce/Conflict) placed in 6th.
-   **Rule:** Dasha of 6th lord or planet in 6th often brings legal separation.
-   **Result:** Finalization of divorce.

### Death (August 31, 1997)
**Dasha:** **Jupiter Mahadasha / Venus Antardasha / Rahu Pratyantardasha**.
-   **Jupiter:** 2nd Lord (Maraka - Killer). Debilitated.
-   **Venus:** 6th Lord in 6th. Dispositor of...
-   **Rahu:** In 9th (Leo) with Mars.
    -   Mars rules 12th (Loss) and 5th.
    -   Rahu gives results of Mars.
    -   **Mars is in Leo:** 8th from the Moon (Aquarius).
    -   **Transit:**
        -   **Solar Eclipse** occurred just before death.
        -   **Saturn** was transiting Pisces (4th house - End of life/Vehicle?).
        -   **Nodes** were transiting Leo/Aquarius axis (Returing to natal position - Nodal Return).
        -   **Rahu/Mars** transit in 8th/9th?
    -   **Vehicle Accident:** Mars (Violence) + Rahu (Suddenness) + 4th House aspect (Vehicle).

## Conclusion

Princess Diana's chart is a poignant example of how planetary positions manifest literally:
1.  **Moon in Aquarius (People's Princess):** Humanitarian, connected to the masses, yet emotionally detached/lonely (Ketu).
2.  **Venus in 6th (Tragic Love):** Love (Venus) found in the house of disease/service (6th). She found love by hugging AIDS patients, not in her marriage.
3.  **7th House Affliction:** Sun (Royal Ego) burnt the relationship.
4.  **Lagna Lord Debilitated:** The self was subsumed and crushed by the weight of the 2nd House (Royal Family lineage).
