# Sankarsh Planetary Progression: D1 → D9 → D10

## How to Read This

Every planet occupies a sign in D1 (your waking life), D9 (your soul), and D10 (your career). But a planet's journey across these three charts is not random — it tells a story of transformation. 

This file uses two lenses:
1. **Direct progression** — what the planet does in each chart's own house system.
2. **Rasi Tulya** — taking the D9/D10 sign and projecting it back onto D1 houses (from Scorpio Ascendant), revealing *where in your physical life* that deeper energy actually lands.

## ☉ Sun

| Chart | Sign | House (Own Chart) | RTN/RTD House (From Scorpio) |
|-------|------|-------------------|------------------------------|
| D1 | Libra (Debilitated) | 12th | — |
| D9 | Sagittarius | 10th | **2nd** |
| D10 | Sagittarius | 9th | **2nd** |

**The Story:**
Your ego begins in a deep valley. Debilitated in the 12th house, the Sun has no stage — it works in isolation, behind curtains, in foreign spaces. There is private self-doubt. The relationship with authority figures and the father is complex.

But the Sun tells the same story in both D9 and D10: **Sagittarius**. The soul and the career both agree — you are meant to be a teacher, a philosopher, a guiding voice. The debilitation is the tuition fee, not the destination.

**Rasi Tulya Insight:** Both RTN and RTD place the Sun in your **2nd house** (wealth, family, speech). Despite all the 12th-house hiding, the Sun's real job is to build your voice, your bank account, and your family legacy. This is where its energy *actually* arrives in your physical life.

## ☽ Moon

| Chart | Sign | House (Own Chart) | RTN/RTD House (From Scorpio) |
|-------|------|-------------------|------------------------------|
| D1 | Sagittarius (Mula) | 2nd | — |
| D9 | Cancer (Own Sign) | 5th | **9th** |
| D10 | Pisces | 12th | **5th** |

**The Story:**
Emotionally, you start as a collector — of values, resources, knowledge, family bonds. The Moon in the 2nd house hoards security. But the soul (D9) is far more expansive — Moon in Cancer in the 5th is one of the warmest placements possible. Creativity, children, romance, learning — this is where the heart truly lives.

In D10, the Moon retreats to the 12th. Professionally, your emotional investment happens quietly. You don't wear your passion on your sleeve at work.

**Rasi Tulya Insight:** RTN throws the Moon into the **9th house** — dharma, luck, and long journeys. Your emotional fulfillment doesn't come from possessions (D1-2nd) but from wisdom and travel. RTD lands it in the **5th** — your career emotional output is creative expression and mentorship. The money-minded Moon of D1 is secretly a philosopher and an artist.

## ♂ Mars

| Chart | Sign | House (Own Chart) | RTN/RTD House (From Scorpio) |
|-------|------|-------------------|------------------------------|
| D1 | Leo | 10th (Digbala) | — |
| D9 | Virgo | 7th | **11th** |
| D10 | Capricorn (Exalted) | 10th | **3rd** |

**The Story:**
Mars is the king of this chart. Lagna lord in the 10th with maximum directional strength — you are born to command. In D9, Mars refines itself. The raw fire of Leo becomes the precise scalpel of Virgo in the 7th — your soul wants partnerships that are analytical, service-driven, and useful. No wasted energy.

Then the crescendo: Mars **exalted** in D10's 10th house. The ambition of the birth chart reaches its absolute ceiling in the career chart. This is the rarest, most dominant career combination.

**Rasi Tulya Insight:** RTN places Mars in the **11th house** — your soul's ambition converts into gains, fulfillment, and large networks. But RTD drops it into the **3rd** — profession demands raw courage, personal initiative, writing, and hustle. You'll earn through networks (11th) but only by putting in hands-on work (3rd). Exalted Mars ensures the effort is wildly disproportionate to the reward — in your favor.

## ☿ Mercury

| D1 | Libra | 12th | — |
| D9 | Gemini (Own Sign) | 4th | **8th** |

**The Story:**
Mercury starts hidden — your intellect works behind closed doors in D1. It processes information in isolation, thinks in foreign languages, and solves problems nobody sees you solving.

In D9, Mercury comes home to Gemini. The soul's thinking is razor-sharp, sitting comfortably in the 4th house — you need intellectual peace and a home full of books and ideas. ## ♃ Jupiter

| Chart | Sign | House (Own Chart) | RTN/RTD House (From Scorpio) |
|-------|------|-------------------|------------------------------|
| D1 | Aquarius (Retrograde) | 4th | — |
| D9 | Taurus | 3rd | **7th** |
| D10 | Libra | 7th | **12th** |

**The Story:**
Jupiter sits quietly at the base of your chart — 4th house, conjunct Ketu, retrograde. Wisdom turned inward. This is the philosopher who reads alone at 3 AM, not the one on stage. In D9, Jupiter moves to the 3rd in Taurus — the soul wants to put wisdom into tangible, practical effort.

D10 places Jupiter in the 7th — professionally, your wisdom is best deployed in partnerships, consulting, and advisory roles.

**Rasi Tulya Insight:** RTN confirms the 7th house — the soul's wisdom is meant for partnerships, not solitude. But RTD pushes Jupiter to the **12th house**. Professionally, your advisory and consulting work happens behind the scenes, in foreign organizations, or in institutional settings. You're the guru nobody sees — and that's precisely where Jupiter is most powerful.

> [!NOTE]
> **The tension:** Your soul wants to share wisdom in partnerships (RTN-7th), but your career deploys it in isolation (RTD-12th). Resolution: you advise and consult for foreign/private entities. The partner sees you; the public doesn't.

## ♄ Saturn

| D1 | Aries (Debilitated, Retrograde) | 6th | — |
| D9 | Taurus | 3rd | **7th** |
| D10 | Gemini | 3rd | **8th** |

**The Story:**
Saturn begins at its weakest — debilitated, retrograde, in the house of enemies and disease. Patience is thin. Health requires active management. Daily grind feels like punishment not purpose.

But Saturn doesn't stay broken. In both D9 and D10 it lands in the 3rd house — efforts, communication, courage. The soul and the career both say the same thing: Saturn's redemption lies in disciplined, patient effort. D9 in Taurus grounds it. D10 in Gemini sharpens its communication.

**Rasi Tulya Insight:** RTN places Saturn in the **7th house** — your deepest lessons in patience come through relationships and partnerships. RTD sends it to the **8th house** — professionally, your discipline is best applied to transformation, crisis management, research, and handling other people's resources. The debilitation forces you to develop muscles that most people never build.

## ☋ Ketu

| D1 | Aquarius | 4th | — |
| D9 | Scorpio | 9th | **1st** |
| D10 | Pisces | 12th | **5th** |

**The Story:**
Ketu in the 4th with Jupiter — detachment from conventional domestic bliss. You don't seek comfort the way most people do. The home is a launchpad, not a resting place.

D9 places Ketu in Scorpio in the 9th — the soul carries past-life mastery of occult, tantra, and deep transformative wisdom. In D10, Ketu reaches the 12th in Pisces — professional liberation. Complete spiritual detachment from career outcomes.

**Rasi Tulya Insight:** RTN sends Ketu to the **1st house** — at the deepest level, *you are Ketu*. Detachment, moksha-seeking, and past-life wisdom aren't just things you do — they are your fundamental identity. RTD places it in the **5th house** — your career's spiritual output is creative intelligence, intuition, and past-life merit (Purva Punya) expressing through your work.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Ketu in the 1st (RTN) is the spiritual anchor of the entire chart.** While Mars and Rahu chase worldly dominance, Ketu quietly ensures that the soul never forgets what it's really here for.

## The Three Convergence Points

When multiple planets project onto the same RTN/RTD house, that house becomes a powerhouse in your life.

| House | What Lands Here | Pattern |
|-------|----------------|---------|
| **2nd** | Sun (RTN+RTD), Venus (RTN+RTD) | Four projections. Wealth, voice, and family are the inevitable destination — no matter how much the 12th house tries to scatter things. |
| **7th** | Jupiter (RTN), Saturn (RTN), Rahu (RTN) | Three soul-level projections. Every major life lesson, alliance, and desire converges in partnerships. Relationships are the furnace of growth. |
| **5th** | Moon (RTD), Ketu (RTD) | Two career projections. The professional output is creative, intuitive, and quietly spiritual — children, students, and creative works are the legacy. |
