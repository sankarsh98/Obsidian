## Detailed Planet Considerations

### Sun in 12th House (Libra)

**Dignity:** Debilitated  
**Lordship:** Lord of 10th House (Career/Status)

The Sun placed in the 12th house indicates:
- Expenditure on foreign lands or travels
- Connection with hospitals, spiritual retreats, or foreign countries
- Father may live far away or have health concerns
- Authority manifests in private/behind-the-scenes matters
- Strong potential for work in foreign lands or multinational organizations

> [!NOTE]
> 10th lord in 12th creates **Raja Yoga for foreign success** - career flourishes abroad.

---

### Moon in 2nd House (Sagittarius - Mula Nakshatra)

**Dignity:** Neutral  
**Lordship:** Lord of 9th House (Fortune/Father)

The Moon in 2nd house brings:
- **Large and happy family** - emotional connection to family wealth
- **Good earnings and savings** - financial stability through Moon's nurturing
- **Sweet speech** - pleasant communication style
- Ups and downs in financial conditions
- Ability to provide employment to others
- **Higher education** - thirst for knowledge
- Attractive and pleasant face
- Some initial obstacles in studies, overcome through concentration

**Moon in Mula Nakshatra traits:**
- Sweet nature and peace-loving
- High faith in justice
- Strong and firm thoughts
- Participates passionately in social works
- Leaves everything to the divine
- Exceptional success in writing, arts, and social sector
- Talent shines away from birthplace
- Success in both job and business, but prefers job
- Interest in spirituality, no greed for money

---

### Mars in 10th House (Leo)

**Dignity:** Friendly Sign (Leo is ruled by Sun, friend of Mars)  
**Lordship:** Lord of 1st House (Self) and 6th House (Enemies/Health)

> [!TIP]
> **Mars in 10th house is one of the most powerful placements for career**

The Mars in 10th house (with Rahu) indicates:
- **Wealth and special skills** - making you famous
- Makes family proud through achievements
- **Different types of vehicles** and enjoyment from them
- Ambitious nature with strong decision-making
- Higher posts and respect in career
- Proficient in surgery, mantras, and technical knowledge
- **Special achievement at age 28**

**Suitable careers:**
- Mechanical Engineering
- Electronic Engineering
- Arms and Ammunition
- Uniforms (Military/Police)
- Leadership positions

**Challenges:**
- May affect younger siblings
- Mother's health may need attention
- Potential differences with life partner

---

### Mercury in 12th House (Libra)

**Dignity:** Friendly Sign  
**Lordship:** Lord of 8th House (Transformation) and 11th House (Gains)

Mercury in 12th house brings mixed results:
- **Intelligent person** with good thinking capacity
- Acts on reasonable grounds
- Firm belief in religious matters
- Sound knowledge of Vedas and scriptures
- Soft-hearted nature toward all
- **Good speaker or teacher potential**
- Defeats enemies through intellect
- Younger paternal uncle will be well-settled
- Acquires property and achieves high positions

**Advice:**
- Stay away from laziness
- Maintain character decency
- Be patient to avoid becoming greedy
- Handle work wisely to gain favors

---

### Jupiter in 4th House (Aquarius) ℞ Retrograde

**Dignity:** Neutral Sign  
**Lordship:** Lord of 2nd House (Wealth) and 5th House (Children/Creativity)

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Jupiter retrograde in 4th house indicates internalized wisdom and unconventional approaches to home/property**

Jupiter in 4th house makes you:
- **Good looking, strong, and intelligent**
- Kind-hearted and brilliant
- Appreciable rhetoric nature
- Ambitious and good-natured
- **Head of the family** - respected by all
- Good entrepreneur and businessman

**Property & Home:**
- Pleasure of various vehicles
- Big home full of luxuries
- Possible government housing or benefits
- Expensive clothing and garlands
- Sufficient property including cattle/animals

**Family:**
- Happiness and affection from mother
- Loving service to parents
- Need to work hard for ancestral property
- Old age will be happier

---

### Venus in 12th House (Libra) - Own Sign

**Dignity:** Svakshetra (Own Sign)  
**Lordship:** Lord of 7th House (Marriage) and 12th House (Expenses/Foreign)

> [!TIP]
> **Venus in own sign in 12th indicates luxury expenditure, foreign comforts, and bed pleasures**

Venus in own sign brings:
- **Luxurious and glorious lifestyle**
- Respect for people with good qualities
- No financial problems
- Prudent spending while not denying comfort
- **Happy conjugal life**
- Early marriage (possibly before expected)
- Attractive to opposite gender
- Large number of friends

**Challenges:**
- Inclination toward sensual activities
- May gain weight with age
- Unnecessary spending on useless things at times
- Differences with close ones
- May not recognize enemies who pose as friends
- Need to maintain respect for spouse

---

### Saturn in 6th House (Aries) ℞ Retrograde (Debilitated)

**Dignity:** Debilitated Sign (Neecha)  
**Lordship:** Lord of 3rd House (Courage) and 4th House (Home)

> [!NOTE]
> **Though debilitated, Saturn in 6th house is considered good (Vipareet position)** - destroys enemies and gives victory in competition.

Saturn in 6th house gives:
- **Physically fit, toned, and healthy**
- Strong and powerful constitution
- Good digestion and appetite
- **Good speaker and logical person**
- Opponents will be afraid of you
- Many friends who receive your favor
- Many servants and followers
- Enemies will respect you
- Fame, money, and authority (if humble)

**Aspects from Saturn:**
- 8th house (Longevity)
- 12th house (Expenses)  
- 3rd house (Courage)

**Advice:**
- Avoid being proudy/arrogant
- Stay away from sensuous acts
- Maintain humility for best results

---

### Rahu in 10th House (Leo)

**Lordship:** —  
**House:** 10th (Career/Status)

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Rahu in 10th is one of the most ambitious placements for worldly success**

Rahu in 10th house makes you:
- **Strong and brave**
- Intelligent and charitable
- Interest in poems and poetry
- Potential as writer or editor
- **Success, glory, respect, and high esteem**
- Officer of groups, village, or city
- Potential to become minister or commander

**Other effects:**
- Opportunity for pilgrimage to holy places
- Organizer of religious events
- Destroys enemies - fewer adversaries
- May have fewer sons
- Expert in business with travel required
- Success in legal issues
- Suffers initially but becomes progressive later

**Advice:**
- Avoid becoming lazy or unenthusiastic
- Complete work regularly
- Avoid pride and addiction

---

### Ketu in 4th House (Aquarius)

**Lordship:** —  
**House:** 4th (Home/Mother/Peace of Mind)

Ketu in 4th house gives:
- **Brave and strong** nature
- Belief in speaking truth
- Soft-spoken mostly
- Flourishing wealth potential

**Challenges:**
- May make you playful and witted
- Careless in work at times
- Lack of zeal
- Tendency to criticize others
- Limited good time with mother (health concerns)
- Sufferings due to friends
- **Paternal property may be destroyed** or not received
- May need to go to foreign country for earning
- Financial problems may cause disturbance
- Fear from poisonous things
