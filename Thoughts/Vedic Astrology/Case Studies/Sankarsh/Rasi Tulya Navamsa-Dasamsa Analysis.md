# Sankarsh Rasi Tulya Navamsa & Rasi Tulya Dasamsa Analysis

## What is Rasi Tulya?

**Rasi Tulya Navamsa (RTN)** and **Rasi Tulya Dasamsa (RTD)** are powerful techniques that map divisional chart placements back onto the Rashi (D1) framework. Instead of reading D9/D10 from their own Ascendants, we take the *sign* a planet occupies in the divisional chart and see which D1 house it falls in — counted from the D1 Ascendant (Scorpio).

This reveals how the deeper layers of your chart (soul purpose, professional destiny) project onto and influence your actual, physical life areas.

- **D1 House:** The physical reality — what happens in the material world.
- **RTN House:** The soul's imprint on your physical life — where deep inner qualities actually manifest.
- **RTD House:** The career imprint on your physical life — where professional energies actually play out.

> [!NOTE]
> **D1 Ascendant: Scorpio.** All houses are counted from Scorpio as the 1st.
> Aries = 6th, Taurus = 7th, Gemini = 8th, Cancer = 9th, Leo = 10th, Virgo = 11th, Libra = 12th, Scorpio = 1st, Sagittarius = 2nd, Capricorn = 3rd, Aquarius = 4th, Pisces = 5th.

## Planet-by-Planet Progression

### ☉ Sun — 12th → 2nd (RTN) → 2nd (RTD)
**D1 Theme:** Hidden, debilitated ego in the house of loss, isolation, and foreign lands.

**RTN (Soul Imprint → 2nd House):**
The soul pulls the Sun's energy out of the shadows and directly into the 2nd house of **family, wealth, speech, and values**. Internally, the Sun's true purpose is not isolation — it is to build a value system, accumulate resources through self-worth, and develop a powerful, authoritative voice. The debilitation in D1 is a test; the soul knows where the real treasure lies.

**RTD (Career Imprint → 2nd House):**
Identical to RTN — a rare and powerful confirmation. Professionally, your career will ultimately serve income generation, building family wealth, and establishing your name through speech or communication. Despite starting in the background (D1-12th), the Sun's career destiny is squarely in the realm of wealth and voice.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **The Sun's double confirmation into the 2nd house is remarkable.** Both the soul and the career agree: your ego and authority must express through earning power, spoken/written word, and foundational value systems — not through public display.

### ☽ Moon — 2nd → 9th (RTN) → 5th (RTD)
**D1 Theme:** Emotional security tied to family, speech, and accumulated wealth.

**RTN (Soul Imprint → 9th House):**
The soul elevates the Moon from material security (2nd) to **dharma, higher learning, and fortune** (9th). Your emotional fulfillment at the deepest level comes not from money, but from philosophical pursuit, long-distance travel, and connecting with gurus or teachers. The Moon in Cancer (own sign) in D9 makes this a very comfortable, natural emotional state.

**RTD (Career Imprint → 5th House):**
Professionally, the Moon's energy manifests in the 5th house of **creativity, intelligence, speculation, and children/students**. Your career emotionally resonates when you are creating, advising, or working in education/mentorship. The emotional well-being from family (D1-2nd) transforms into professional creativity (RTD-5th).

> [!TIP]
> **The arc is beautiful:** Material comfort (D1) → Spiritual wisdom (RTN) → Creative expression (RTD). The Moon matures from possessing to seeking to creating.

### ♂ Mars — 10th → 11th (RTN) → 3rd (RTD)
**D1 Theme:** Lagna lord with Digbala in the house of career, power, and public action.

**RTN (Soul Imprint → 11th House):**
The soul channels Mars from pure career ambition (10th) into the 11th house of **gains, networks, large organizations, and fulfilled desires**. The internal Mars doesn't just want power — it wants the *fruits* of that power: income, networks, elder sibling-like mentors, and the realization of long-held ambitions.

**RTD (Career Imprint → 3rd House):**
In the professional chart, Mars lands in the 3rd house of **courage, hands-on effort, communication, and short travels**. This is Mars in Capricorn (Exalted) projecting into the house of action. Your career success is not handed to you — it is built through relentless personal effort, bold communication, writing, and courageous initiatives. You must physically act, travel, and hustle.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **The progression tells a complete story:** You achieve career dominance (D1-10th) → which converts into massive gains and network (RTN-11th) → but only through ground-level courage and personal effort (RTD-3rd). The exalted Mars in D10 ensures that these efforts yield disproportionate results.

### ☿ Mercury — 12th → 8th (RTN) → 9th (RTD)
**D1 Theme:** Intellect operating in seclusion — foreign analysis, behind-the-scenes thinking.

**RTN (Soul Imprint → 8th House):**
The soul drives Mercury deep into the 8th house of **transformation, occult, research, and hidden knowledge**. Your inner intellect is wired for investigation — cracking codes, probing beneath the surface, understanding what others cannot see. This is the hallmark of a researcher, detective, or deep analyst. Mercury in its own sign (Gemini) in D9 makes this a fearfully sharp, articulate analytical mind.

**RTD (Career Imprint → 9th House):**
Professionally, Mercury projects into the 9th house of **higher education, philosophy, law, publishing, and dharma**. Your communication skills are destined to be used in teaching, mentoring, or writing about higher-order subjects. The hidden researcher (RTN-8th) publishes and teaches (RTD-9th).

> [!TIP]
> **The arc:** Hidden intellect (D1-12th) → Transforms into deep research capability (RTN-8th) → Manifests as published wisdom and higher teaching (RTD-9th).

### ♃ Jupiter — 4th → 7th (RTN) → 12th (RTD)
**D1 Theme:** Wisdom, peace, and spiritual knowledge rooted in the home/inner mind. Conjunct Ketu.

**RTN (Soul Imprint → 7th House):**
The soul pushes Jupiter from private, inner wisdom into the 7th house of **partnerships, marriage, and public dealings**. Your deepest wisdom is meant to be *shared* — not hoarded. The soul craves a wise, meaningful partnership (romantic or business) where both parties elevate each other through knowledge.

**RTD (Career Imprint → 12th House):**
Professionally, Jupiter projects into the 12th house of **foreign lands, isolation, expenditure, and spiritual liberation**. Your professional wisdom is spent behind the scenes — consulting for foreign entities, working in research labs, or serving in institutions (hospitals, ashrams, universities abroad). Jupiter's wisdom doesn't seek the spotlight; it serves silently.

> [!WARNING]
> **Jupiter's arc reveals a tension:** Inner wisdom seeks partnership (RTN-7th) but career wisdom leads to isolation/foreign work (RTD-12th). The resolution is that your *personal* relationships bring wisdom, but your *professional* application of that wisdom happens in private, foreign, or institutional settings.

### ♀ Venus — 12th → 2nd (RTN) → 2nd (RTD)
**D1 Theme:** Love, luxury, and relationships in the house of foreign lands and expenditure. Venus is in its own sign (Libra-Moolatrikona), so it is strong despite the 12th house placement.

**RTN (Soul Imprint → 2nd House):**
Exactly like the Sun — the soul pulls Venus out of the 12th house shadows and into the 2nd house of **family, accumulated wealth, and speech**. Your deepest sense of love and beauty is expressed through your voice, your family bonds, and your ability to accumulate beautiful things. The soul's Venus is not about escapism — it's about building a beautiful, harmonious home base.

**RTD (Career Imprint → 2nd House):**
Again, identical to RTN. Professionally, Venus is about earning. Your creative, diplomatic, and aesthetic talents are tools for generating income, building family wealth, and cultivating a refined professional voice.

> [!NOTE]
> **Both Sun and Venus share the exact same RTN-RTD arc (12th → 2nd → 2nd).** Since they are conjunct in D1 (both in Libra-12th), conjunct in D9 (both in Sagittarius), and conjunct in D10 (both in Sagittarius), they are deeply intertwined. Your ego (Sun) and your love/creativity (Venus) operate as one unit — always pulling you from hidden isolation toward visible wealth and voice.

### ♄ Saturn — 6th → 7th (RTN) → 8th (RTD)
**D1 Theme:** Debilitated discipline in the house of enemies, disease, and daily struggle.

**RTN (Soul Imprint → 7th House):**
The soul takes Saturn's harsh lessons from daily grind (6th) and channels them into the 7th house of **partnerships and marriage**. This indicates that patience, discipline, and endurance are deeply tied to relationships. Partnerships will test you, require maturity, and likely involve elder or serious individuals. The soul learns its hardest lessons through commitment to others.

**RTD (Career Imprint → 8th House):**
Professionally, Saturn projects into the 8th house of **longevity, transformation, insurance, inheritance, and other people's resources**. Your disciplined work ethic finds its best professional expression in managing transformations — corporate restructuring, financial auditing, insurance, research into hidden systems, or dealing with crises. Saturn here gives immense professional longevity.

> [!WARNING]
> **Saturn's debilitation in D1 is redeemed through this arc.** The initial frustration of daily enemies and health battles (D1-6th) forces the development of patience in partnerships (RTN-7th) which then becomes a formidable professional tool for handling deep, transformative, and crisis-driven work (RTD-8th).

### ☊ Rahu — 10th → 7th (RTN) → 11th (RTD)
**D1 Theme:** Obsessive, unconventional ambition in the career house. Conjunct Mars.

**RTN (Soul Imprint → 7th House):**
Internally, Rahu's obsession shifts from career to **partnerships**. The soul is hungry for meaningful, perhaps foreign or unconventional, alliances. There is a deep fascination with "the other" — business partners, spouse, or public audiences that seem exotic or outside your comfort zone.

**RTD (Career Imprint → 11th House):**
Professionally, Rahu projects into the 11th house of **massive gains, large networks, elder mentors, and fulfilled desires**. This is an extremely favorable RTD placement — the obsessive career drive (D1-10th) translates directly into the house of gains (RTD-11th). Unconventional professional networks, tech communities, and large-scale organizations become the vehicles for realizing your deepest ambitions.

> [!TIP]
> **Rahu's arc is pure ambition optimized:** Career obsession (D1-10th) → Foreign/unconventional partnerships (RTN-7th) → Massive gains and network fulfillment (RTD-11th). The desire doesn't dissipate — it multiplies and converts into tangible results.

### ☋ Ketu — 4th → 1st (RTN) → 5th (RTD)
**D1 Theme:** Spiritual detachment from home, motherland, and inner peace. Conjunct Jupiter.

**RTN (Soul Imprint → 1st House):**
This is deeply significant. The soul brings Ketu directly to the **1st house — the Self**. At the deepest level, you are Ketu. Detachment, past-life wisdom, and spiritual seeking are not just activities — they are your core identity. The soul is fundamentally a seeker, a monk, a researcher who has already transcended many earthly attachments in previous lives.

**RTD (Career Imprint → 5th House):**
Professionally, Ketu projects into the 5th house of **creativity, intelligence, children/students, and past-life merit (Purva Punya)**. Your professional detachment expresses as intuitive creativity — you may excel at work that requires letting go of conventional logic and tapping into pure inspiration. Teaching, counseling, or creative problem-solving come naturally because you approach them without attachment to outcomes.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Ketu's arc is the spiritual spine of the chart:** Detachment from home (D1-4th) → Becomes the core identity itself (RTN-1st) → Manifests as intuitive, merit-based creativity in career (RTD-5th). This is the signature of someone whose past-life spiritual accumulation directly fuels their present-life intelligence and professional creativity.

### The Three Pillars of This Chart

1. **Earn and Speak (2nd House Dominance):** Four planetary projections (Sun + Venus, both RTN and RTD) converge on the 2nd house. Despite starting life in isolation and foreign lands (12th house emphasis in D1), the ultimate destination of both soul and career is wealth accumulation and powerful speech.

2. **Transform Through Partnerships (7th House Dominance):** Three soul-level projections (Jupiter, Saturn, Rahu) converge on the 7th house. The biggest growth, the hardest lessons, and the most obsessive desires all play out through relationships — business, romantic, or public-facing.

3. **Create From Spirit (5th House in RTD):** Moon and Ketu both project into the 5th house professionally. The career's emotional and spiritual output is pure creativity — intuitive, past-life-fueled, and emotionally resonant.
