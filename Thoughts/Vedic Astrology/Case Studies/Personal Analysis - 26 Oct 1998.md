## 4. Comprehensive Yoga Analysis (योग विचार, Yoga Vichara)

### Primary Yogas (Major Combinations)

#### 1. Neecha Bhanga Raja Yoga (Debilitation Cancellation Royal Combination)

**Formation:**
- Sun (10th Lord) is debilitated in Libra (12th house)
- Venus (Lord of Libra, Sun's debilitation sign) is in own sign in the same house
- This creates complete cancellation of Sun's debilitation

**Classical Reference:**
> "Nicha svasthane nicha rashyesho yadi kevalam,
> Neecha bhangam tatha proktam raja yoga karakam."
> (If the lord of the debilitation sign is in its own sign, debilitation is cancelled and Raja Yoga forms.)

**Effects:**
- Career starts with challenges but ultimately achieves royal status
- Recognition comes after initial struggle (mid-20s onward)
- Success in foreign lands more likely than homeland
- Leadership abilities recognized after proving oneself
- Sun Mahadasha (current) will trigger this yoga's activation
