# Planets (Grahas)

In Vedic Astrology, **Grahas** (Sanskrit: ग्रह, "seizers" or "influencers") refer to the nine celestial bodies that exert significant influence on human life. These are collectively known as the **Navagrahas** (nine planets). Unlike modern astronomy, Vedic astrology includes two lunar nodes (Rahu and Ketu) and excludes the outer planets (Uranus, Neptune, Pluto).

## The Navagrahas (Nine Planets)

### 1. Surya (Sun) - The Soul

**Sanskrit**: सूर्य (Sūrya)

**Significations**:
- Soul, consciousness, vitality
- Father, authority figures, government
- Power, status, self-confidence
- Leadership, individuality, ego
- Health, bones, right eye

**Associations**:
- **Element**: Fire (Agni)
- **Gender**: Masculine
- **Caste**: Royal (Kshatriya)
- **Day**: Sunday
- **Direction**: East
- **Metal**: Gold, copper
- **Gemstone**: Ruby
- **Color**: Deep red, orange
- **Deity**: Surya Deva

**Exaltation**: 10° Aries
**Debilitation**: 10° Libra
**Own Signs**: Leo
**Friendly Signs**: Moon, Mars, Jupiter
**Enemy Signs**: Venus, Saturn

The Sun represents the eternal Self (Atman) and one's essential life force. A strong Sun grants confidence, leadership abilities, and vitality, while a weak or afflicted Sun may cause ego issues, health problems, or strained relationship with father.

### 2. Chandra (Moon) - The Mind

**Sanskrit**: चन्द्र (Candra)

**Significations**:
- Mind, emotions, feelings
- Mother, motherhood, nurturing
- Memory, imagination, intuition
- Public, masses, popularity
- Fluids, left eye, blood

**Associations**:
- **Element**: Water (Jala)
- **Gender**: Feminine
- **Caste**: Merchant (Vaishya)
- **Day**: Monday
- **Direction**: Northwest
- **Metal**: Silver, white metals
- **Gemstone**: Pearl, moonstone
- **Color**: White, cream
- **Deity**: Chandra Deva

**Exaltation**: 3° Taurus
**Debilitation**: 3° Scorpio
**Own Sign**: Cancer
**Friendly**: Sun, Mercury
**Enemy**: None (Rahu/Ketu are considered adversaries)

The Moon governs the mind and emotions. Its placement and phase at birth deeply influence mental stability, emotional responses, and the ability to connect with others. The Moon's [[Nakshatras|Nakshatra]] position is particularly significant.

### 3. Mangal (Mars) - Energy and Action

**Sanskrit**: मंगल (Maṅgala)

**Significations**:
- Energy, courage, aggression
- Siblings (especially younger brothers)
- Land, property, real estate
- Military, police, surgeons
- Accidents, injuries, blood
- Sports, physical strength

**Associations**:
- **Element**: Fire (Agni)
- **Gender**: Masculine
- **Caste**: Warrior (Kshatriya)
- **Day**: Tuesday
- **Direction**: South
- **Metal**: Copper, iron
- **Gemstone**: Red coral
- **Color**: Red, crimson
- **Deity**: Kartikeya (Skanda)

**Exaltation**: 28° Capricorn
**Debilitation**: 28° Cancer
**Own Signs**: Aries, Scorpio
**Friendly**: Sun, Moon, Jupiter
**Enemy**: Mercury

Mars provides drive, ambition, and the ability to take action. Well-placed Mars gives courage and determination; afflicted Mars can cause anger, violence, accidents, or conflicts. Mars plays a crucial role in marriage compatibility (Mangal Dosha).

### 4. Budha (Mercury) - Intellect

**Sanskrit**: बुध (Budha)

**Significations**:
- Intellect, communication, speech
- Business, commerce, trade
- Learning, education, writing
- Adaptability, cleverness
- Nervous system, skin

**Associations**:
- **Element**: Earth (Prithvi)
- **Gender**: Neutral (eunuch in classics)
- **Caste**: Merchant (Vaishya)
- **Day**: Wednesday
- **Direction**: North
- **Metal**: Bronze, mixed metals
- **Gemstone**: Emerald
- **Color**: Green
- **Deity**: Vishnu

**Exaltation**: 15° Virgo
**Debilitation**: 15° Pisces
**Own Signs**: Gemini, Virgo
**Friendly**: Sun, Venus
**Enemy**: Moon

Mercury governs rational thinking, communication skills, and commercial abilities. Strong Mercury produces excellent writers, speakers, mathematicians, and businesspeople. Afflicted Mercury may cause speech impediments, anxiety, or deception.

### 5. Guru (Jupiter) - Wisdom and Expansion

**Sanskrit**: गुरु (Guru) or बृहस्पति (Bṛhaspati)

**Significations**:
- Wisdom, knowledge, spirituality
- Teachers, gurus, priests
- Children, especially sons
- Wealth, prosperity, expansion
- Dharma, ethics, justice
- Liver, fat tissue

**Associations**:
- **Element**: Ether (Akasha)
- **Gender**: Masculine
- **Caste**: Brahmin (priestly)
- **Day**: Thursday
- **Direction**: Northeast
- **Metal**: Gold
- **Gemstone**: Yellow sapphire
- **Color**: Yellow, golden
- **Deity**: Brihaspati (Deva Guru)

**Exaltation**: 5° Cancer
**Debilitation**: 5° Capricorn
**Own Signs**: Sagittarius, Pisces
**Friendly**: Sun, Moon, Mars
**Enemy**: Mercury, Venus

Jupiter is the most benefic planet, bringing wisdom, prosperity, and spiritual growth. It protects and expands whatever it touches. Strong Jupiter grants knowledge, children, and wealth. Weak Jupiter may cause lack of wisdom, childlessness, or poverty.

### 6. Shukra (Venus) - Love and Beauty

**Sanskrit**: शुक्र (Śukra)

**Significations**:
- Love, romance, relationships
- Beauty, arts, creativity
- Luxury, comforts, pleasures
- Wife (in male chart), marriage
- Vehicles, jewelry, fashion
- Reproductive system, semen

**Associations**:
- **Element**: Water (Jala)
- **Gender**: Feminine
- **Caste**: Brahmin (priestly)
- **Day**: Friday
- **Direction**: Southeast
- **Metal**: Silver, platinum
- **Gemstone**: Diamond, white sapphire
- **Color**: White, pastel colors
- **Deity**: Lakshmi (goddess of wealth)

**Exaltation**: 27° Pisces
**Debilitation**: 27° Virgo
**Own Signs**: Taurus, Libra
**Friendly**: Mercury, Saturn
**Enemy**: Sun, Moon

Venus governs love, beauty, and material pleasures. Well-placed Venus provides artistic talents, loving relationships, and material comforts. Afflicted Venus may cause relationship troubles, indulgence, or moral weakness.

### 7. Shani (Saturn) - Discipline and Karma

**Sanskrit**: शनि (Śani)

**Significations**:
- Discipline, hard work, service
- Delays, obstacles, restrictions
- Karma, past life debts
- Longevity, old age, death
- Servants, workers, masses
- Bones, teeth, chronic diseases

**Associations**:
- **Element**: Air (Vayu)
- **Gender**: Neutral
- **Caste**: Servant (Shudra)
- **Day**: Saturday
- **Direction**: West
- **Metal**: Iron, steel
- **Gemstone**: Blue sapphire
- **Color**: Black, dark blue
- **Deity**: Yama (god of death/dharma)

**Exaltation**: 20° Libra
**Debilitation**: 20° Aries
**Own Signs**: Capricorn, Aquarius
**Friendly**: Mercury, Venus
**Enemy**: Sun, Moon, Mars

Saturn is the karmic teacher, bringing discipline through hardship. It rewards hard work and punishes shortcuts. Strong Saturn grants longevity, discipline, and eventual success. Afflicted Saturn causes delays, depression, poverty, or isolation. Saturn's transit (Sade Sati, Dhaiya) are particularly significant periods.

### 8. Rahu (North Node) - Worldly Desires

**Sanskrit**: राहु (Rāhu)

**Significations**:
- Worldly ambitions, obsessions
- Foreign lands, unusual paths
- Technology, innovation, illusions
- Sudden events, unconventional
- Poison, addictions, phobias
- Paternal grandfather

**Associations**:
- **Element**: Air (Vayu)
- **Gender**: Feminine
- **Caste**: Outcaste (Chandala)
- **Day**: Saturday (shares with Saturn)
- **Direction**: Southwest
- **Gemstone**: Hessonite (Gomed)
- **Color**: Smoky, multicolor
- **Deity**: Durga

**Exaltation**: Taurus/Gemini (varies by source)
**Debilitation**: Scorpio/Sagittarius
**Friendly**: Mercury, Venus, Saturn
**Enemy**: Sun, Moon, Mars

Rahu represents worldly desires and unconventional paths. It amplifies the house and sign it occupies. Well-placed Rahu can bring foreign success, technological proficiency, or innovative thinking. Afflicted Rahu causes confusion, obsession, addiction, or deception.

### 9. Ketu (South Node) - Spirituality and Liberation

**Sanskrit**: केतु (Ketu)

**Significations**:
- Spirituality, moksha (liberation)
- Past life karma, detachment
- Occult knowledge, mysticism
- Sudden losses, isolation
- Maternal grandfather
- Research, investigation

**Associations**:
- **Element**: Fire (Agni)
- **Gender**: Neutral
- **Caste**: Outcaste (Chandala)
- **Day**: Tuesday (shares with Mars)
- **Direction**: None specific
- **Gemstone**: Cat's eye (Lehsuniya)
- **Color**: Grey, smoky
- **Deity**: Ganesha

**Exaltation**: Scorpio/Sagittarius (varies)
**Debilitation**: Taurus/Gemini
**Friendly**: Mercury, Venus, Saturn
**Enemy**: Sun, Moon, Mars

Ketu represents spiritual liberation and past-life wisdom. It brings detachment from the house it occupies. Well-placed Ketu grants spiritual insight, intuition, and liberation. Afflicted Ketu causes confusion, isolation, or losses. Ketu's influence is subtle but profound.

## Planetary Relationships

### Natural Friendships and Enmities

Planets have inherent relationships affecting their behavior in a chart. These are based on ancient classifications and philosophical principles.

### Functional Benefics and Malefics

The benefic or malefic nature also depends on the [[Houses (Bhavas)|house]] lordship for a given Ascendant, creating functional relationships beyond natural ones.

**Natural Benefics**: Jupiter, Venus, waxing Moon, well-placed Mercury
**Natural Malefics**: Sun, Mars, Saturn, Rahu, Ketu, waning Moon, afflicted Mercury

## Planetary Strengths

Planets gain or lose strength based on:

1. **Sign Placement** (exaltation, own sign, friendly sign, etc.)
2. **House Position** (angular, succedent, cadent)
3. **Aspects Received** (from benefics or malefics)
4. **Retrograde Motion** (appearing to move backward)
5. **Combustion** (too close to Sun)
6. **Planetary War** (conjunction of two planets)
7. **Shadbala** (six-fold strength calculation)

## Using Planetary Knowledge

Understanding the Grahas is essential for:

- Interpreting the [[Birth Chart (Kundali)]]
- Analyzing [[Planetary Periods (Dashas)]]
- Identifying [[Yogas]]
- Prescribing [[Remedies]]
- Understanding transits and their effects

Each planet's placement in [[Zodiac Signs (Rashis)]], [[Houses (Bhavas)]], and [[Nakshatras]] creates a unique influence that must be synthesized for accurate astrological analysis.
