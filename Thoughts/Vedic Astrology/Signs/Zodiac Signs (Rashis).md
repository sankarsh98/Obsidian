# Zodiac Signs (Rashis)

In Vedic Astrology, the **Rashis** (Sanskrit: राशि, "heap" or "collection") are the 12 zodiac signs that form a complete circle of 360 degrees around the Earth. Each sign occupies 30 degrees and represents distinct qualities, elements, and characteristics that color the expression of [[Planets (Grahas)]] placed within them.

## The Sidereal Zodiac

Vedic astrology uses the **sidereal zodiac**, which is based on the actual astronomical positions of constellations in the sky. This differs from Western astrology's tropical zodiac, which is aligned with seasons. Due to the precession of equinoxes, the sidereal zodiac is currently about 24 degrees behind the tropical zodiac (this difference is called Ayanamsa).

## The 12 Rashis

### 1. Mesha (Aries)

**Symbol**: Ram
**Element**: Fire (Agni)
**Mode**: Movable/Cardinal (Chara)
**Ruler**: Mars
**Degrees**: 0° - 30°
**Body Parts**: Head, brain

**Characteristics**:
- Independent, pioneering, courageous
- Impulsive, aggressive, competitive
- Leadership qualities, initiator
- Quick-tempered but quick to forgive

### 2. Vrishabha (Taurus)

**Symbol**: Bull
**Element**: Earth (Prithvi)
**Mode**: Fixed (Sthira)
**Ruler**: Venus
**Degrees**: 30° - 60°
**Body Parts**: Face, throat, neck

**Characteristics**:
- Stable, practical, materialistic
- Patient, persistent, stubborn
- Love of beauty, comfort, luxury
- Strong determination, possessive

### 3. Mithuna (Gemini)

**Symbol**: Couple/Twins
**Element**: Air (Vayu)
**Mode**: Dual/Mutable (Dvisvabhava)
**Ruler**: Mercury
**Degrees**: 60° - 90°
**Body Parts**: Shoulders, arms, hands

**Characteristics**:
- Intellectual, communicative, curious
- Versatile, adaptable, restless
- Social, witty, youthful
- Dual nature, sometimes superficial

### 4. Karka (Cancer)

**Symbol**: Crab
**Element**: Water (Jala)
**Mode**: Movable/Cardinal (Chara)
**Ruler**: Moon
**Degrees**: 90° - 120°
**Body Parts**: Chest, breasts, stomach

**Characteristics**:
- Emotional, sensitive, nurturing
- Intuitive, protective, moody
- Home-loving, attached to past
- Imaginative, sympathetic

### 5. Simha (Leo)

**Symbol**: Lion
**Element**: Fire (Agni)
**Mode**: Fixed (Sthira)
**Ruler**: Sun
**Degrees**: 120° - 150°
**Body Parts**: Heart, upper back

**Characteristics**:
- Confident, dignified, authoritative
- Creative, generous, dramatic
- Leadership qualities, proud
- Noble, sometimes arrogant

### 6. Kanya (Virgo)

**Symbol**: Virgin/Maiden
**Element**: Earth (Prithvi)
**Mode**: Dual/Mutable (Dvisvabhava)
**Ruler**: Mercury
**Degrees**: 150° - 180°
**Body Parts**: Digestive system, intestines

**Characteristics**:
- Analytical, perfectionist, critical
- Service-oriented, practical
- Detail-oriented, health-conscious
- Modest, discriminating

### 7. Tula (Libra)

**Symbol**: Balance/Scales
**Element**: Air (Vayu)
**Mode**: Movable/Cardinal (Chara)
**Ruler**: Venus
**Degrees**: 180° - 210°
**Body Parts**: Kidneys, lower back

**Characteristics**:
- Balanced, diplomatic, harmonious
- Social, partnership-oriented
- Artistic, refined, indecisive
- Justice-seeking, fair-minded

### 8. Vrishchika (Scorpio)

**Symbol**: Scorpion
**Element**: Water (Jala)
**Mode**: Fixed (Sthira)
**Ruler**: Mars
**Degrees**: 210° - 240°
**Body Parts**: Reproductive organs

**Characteristics**:
- Intense, passionate, mysterious
- Transformative, powerful, secretive
- Determined, resourceful, vengeful
- Deep emotions, investigative

### 9. Dhanu (Sagittarius)

**Symbol**: Bow and Arrow/Archer
**Element**: Fire (Agni)
**Mode**: Dual/Mutable (Dvisvabhava)
**Ruler**: Jupiter
**Degrees**: 240° - 270°
**Body Parts**: Thighs, hips

**Characteristics**:
- Philosophical, optimistic, freedom-loving
- Adventurous, truth-seeking
- Generous, direct, sometimes tactless
- Higher learning, spiritual

### 10. Makara (Capricorn)

**Symbol**: Crocodile/Sea-goat
**Element**: Earth (Prithvi)
**Mode**: Movable/Cardinal (Chara)
**Ruler**: Saturn
**Degrees**: 270° - 300°
**Body Parts**: Knees, bones

**Characteristics**:
- Ambitious, disciplined, practical
- Patient, responsible, conservative
- Hard-working, status-conscious
- Traditional, sometimes pessimistic

### 11. Kumbha (Aquarius)

**Symbol**: Water-bearer
**Element**: Air (Vayu)
**Mode**: Fixed (Sthira)
**Ruler**: Saturn
**Degrees**: 300° - 330°
**Body Parts**: Calves, ankles

**Characteristics**:
- Humanitarian, innovative, unconventional
- Intellectual, detached, friendly
- Progressive, independent
- Scientific, sometimes aloof

### 12. Meena (Pisces)

**Symbol**: Fish (pair)
**Element**: Water (Jala)
**Mode**: Dual/Mutable (Dvisvabhava)
**Ruler**: Jupiter
**Degrees**: 330° - 360°
**Body Parts**: Feet

**Characteristics**:
- Compassionate, intuitive, spiritual
- Dreamy, imaginative, escapist
- Sensitive, artistic, sacrificing
- Universal love, sometimes confused

## Sign Classifications

### By Element (Tattva)

**Fire Signs** (Agni): Aries, Leo, Sagittarius
- Energy, enthusiasm, initiative
- Action-oriented, passionate

**Earth Signs** (Prithvi): Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn
- Practical, stable, material
- Grounded, methodical

**Air Signs** (Vayu): Gemini, Libra, Aquarius
- Intellectual, communicative, social
- Mental, logical

**Water Signs** (Jala): Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces
- Emotional, intuitive, sensitive
- Feeling, flowing

### By Mode (Gunas)

**Cardinal/Movable Signs** (Chara Rashis): Aries, Cancer, Libra, Capricorn
- Initiating, active, changeable
- Directional movement and travel

**Fixed Signs** (Sthira Rashis): Taurus, Leo, Scorpio, Aquarius
- Stable, determined, resistant to change
- Consolidation and persistence

**Mutable/Dual Signs** (Dvisvabhava Rashis): Gemini, Virgo, Sagittarius, Pisces
- Adaptable, flexible, dual-natured
- Versatility and transformation

### By Gender

**Masculine/Odd Signs**: Aries, Gemini, Leo, Libra, Sagittarius, Aquarius
- Active, outgoing, expressive
- Yang energy

**Feminine/Even Signs**: Taurus, Cancer, Virgo, Scorpio, Capricorn, Pisces
- Receptive, introspective, nurturing
- Yin energy

### By Nature

**Benefic Signs**: Generally, signs ruled by benefic planets (Venus, Jupiter, Mercury)
**Malefic Signs**: Signs ruled by malefic planets (Mars, Saturn)

## Planetary Dignity in Signs

### Exaltation (Uchcha)
Maximum planetary strength:
- Sun: 10° Aries
- Moon: 3° Taurus
- Mars: 28° Capricorn
- Mercury: 15° Virgo
- Jupiter: 5° Cancer
- Venus: 27° Pisces
- Saturn: 20° Libra

### Debilitation (Neecha)
Minimum planetary strength (opposite sign of exaltation):
- Sun: 10° Libra
- Moon: 3° Scorpio
- Mars: 28° Cancer
- Mercury: 15° Pisces
- Jupiter: 5° Capricorn
- Venus: 27° Virgo
- Saturn: 20° Aries

### Own Signs (Sva-Rashi)
Planets are strong and comfortable in their own signs:
- Sun: Leo
- Moon: Cancer
- Mars: Aries, Scorpio
- Mercury: Gemini, Virgo
- Jupiter: Sagittarius, Pisces
- Venus: Taurus, Libra
- Saturn: Capricorn, Aquarius

### Moolatrikona
Special dignity between exaltation and own sign:
- Sun: 0°-20° Leo
- Moon: 4°-30° Taurus
- Mars: 0°-12° Aries
- Mercury: 16°-20° Virgo
- Jupiter: 0°-10° Sagittarius
- Venus: 0°-15° Libra
- Saturn: 0°-20° Aquarius

## Sign Combinations

### Kendra (Angles)
1st, 4th, 7th, 10th houses from Ascendant
Cardinal signs when Aries rises

### Trikona (Trines)
1st, 5th, 9th houses from Ascendant
Fire signs when Aries rises

### Dusthana (Difficult Houses)
6th, 8th, 12th houses from Ascendant

## Using Sign Knowledge

Understanding Rashis is crucial for:

- Interpreting planetary placements in the [[Birth Chart (Kundali)]]
- Analyzing [[Houses (Bhavas)]] through sign qualities
- Determining planetary strength and dignity
- Understanding compatibility (synastry)
- Analyzing [[Nakshatras]] which are subdivisions of signs
- Calculating [[Divisional Charts]]

The sign a planet occupies significantly modifies how that planet's energy expresses itself, creating the unique tapestry of an individual's astrological blueprint.
