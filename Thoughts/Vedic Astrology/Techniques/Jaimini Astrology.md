# Jaimini Astrology (Jaimini Sutras)

## Introduction

**Jaimini Astrology** is a specialized branch of Vedic Astrology based on the **Jaimini Sutras** attributed to Sage Jaimini (a disciple of Vyasa). While the standard system (Parashari) relies on fixed planetary values, Jaimini introduces a dynamic, sign-based system.

**Key Differences from Parashari:**
1.  **Aspects:** In Parashari, *Planets* aspect Planets. In Jaimini, **Signs (Rashis)** aspect Signs (carrying the planets inside them).
2.  **Dashas:** Parashari uses Planetary Dashas (Vimshottari). Jaimini uses **Rashi Dashas** (Chara Dasha).
3.  **Karakas:** Parashari uses fixed significators (e.g., Sun = Father). Jaimini uses **Chara Karakas** (Movable Significators) based on planetary degrees.

## The Chara Karakas (Movable Significators)

In Jaimini, the 7 planets (excluding Rahu/Ketu) become significators based on their longitude in a sign (0° - 30°), from highest to lowest degree.

| Name | Abbr | Rank (Degree) | Signification | Parashari Equivalent |
|------|------|---------------|---------------|----------------------|
| **Atmakaraka** | **AK** | Highest Deg | **The Soul / Self / King** | Sun / Lagna |
| **Amatyakaraka** | **AmK** | 2nd Highest | **Career / Status / Minister** | 10th Lord |
| **Bhratrukaraka**| **BK** | 3rd Highest | **Siblings / Guru / Father** | Mars / 3rd/9th |
| **Matrukaraka** | **MK** | 4th Highest | **Mother / Home / Property** | Moon / 4th |
| **Putrakaraka** | **PK** | 5th Highest | **Children / Creativity** | Jupiter / 5th |
| **Gnatikaraka** | **GK** | 6th Highest | **Enemies / Diseases / Rivals**| Saturn / 6th |
| **Darakaraka** | **DK** | Lowest Deg | **Spouse / Partners** | Venus / 7th |

*(Note: Some traditions include Rahu and use 8 Karakas. The 7 Karaka system is most common).*

### The Atmakaraka (AK) - The King

The planet with the highest degree in any sign is the **Atmakaraka**.
*   It is the most important planet in the chart.
*   It represents the soul's desire and the main karmic lesson of this life.
*   **Karakamsa:** The sign occupied by the AK in the Navamsa (D-9) chart. This is treated as a special Ascendant for spiritual analysis.

## Jaimini Aspects (Rashi Drishti)

Signs aspect each other according to their modality (Movable, Fixed, Dual). Planets inside these signs "ride" the aspect.

1.  **Movable Signs (Aries, Cancer, Libra, Cap):** Aspect all **Fixed Signs** (except the one adjacent to them).
    *   *Aries aspects Leo, Scorpio, Aquarius.* (Skips Taurus).
2.  **Fixed Signs (Taurus, Leo, Scorpio, Aqu):** Aspect all **Movable Signs** (except the one adjacent to them).
    *   *Taurus aspects Cancer, Libra, Capricorn.* (Skips Aries).
3.  **Dual Signs (Gemini, Virgo, Sag, Pisces):** Aspect **each other**.
    *   *Gemini aspects Virgo, Sagittarius, Pisces.*

## Chara Dasha (Sign-Based Timing)

Unlike Vimshottari (which is uniform for everyone), Chara Dasha sequences depend on the nature of the Rising Sign.

*   **Order:**
    *   Aries Lagna: Aries, Taurus, Gemini... (Direct)
    *   Taurus Lagna: Taurus, Aries, Pisces... (Reverse)
    *   *Complex rules apply for specific signs.*
*   **Duration:** Determined by counting from the Sign to its Lord. (Example: If Aries Lord [Mars] is in Gemini [3rd house], duration is 3 - 1 = 2 years).

**Usage:** Excellent for timing career changes (AmK period) and marriage (DK period).

## Jaimini Yogas (Raja Yogas)

Jaimini has its own unique Raja Yogas formed by the connection of Chara Karakas.

1.  **AK - AmK Connection:** Connection between Soul (AK) and Career (AmK). The highest Raja Yoga.
2.  **AK - PK Connection:** Soul + Creativity/Intelligence.
3.  **AmK - PK Connection:** Career + Intelligence.
4.  **AK - DK Connection:** Soul + Spouse (often leads to business partnerships).

**Connection methods:**
*   Conjunction in same sign.
*   Mutual Aspect (Jaimini Aspect).

## Pada (Arudha) Lagna

Jaimini emphasizes the **Arudha** (Illusion/Reflection) of houses.
*   **Calculation:** As far as the Lord is from the House, count that far again from the Lord.
*   **Usage:**
    *   **Lagna (Ascendant):** The True Self.
    *   **Arudha Lagna (AL):** The Maya/Image. How the world perceives you.
    *   *Example:* A person may be poor (weak Lagna) but appear rich (strong Arudha Lagna).
