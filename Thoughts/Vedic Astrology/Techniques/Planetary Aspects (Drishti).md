# Planetary Aspects (Drishti)

## Introduction

In Vedic Astrology, **Drishti** (दृष्टि) means "sight" or "glance" and refers to the way [[Planets (Grahas)]] aspect (influence) other planets and [[Houses (Bhavas)]] in the [[Birth Chart (Kundali)]]. Unlike Western astrology which uses angular aspects (trine, square, etc.), Vedic astrology employs a house-based aspect system where planets "see" specific houses from their position.

**Fundamental Principle:**  
> "यत्र यत्र ग्रहः पश्यति तत्र तत्र फलं ददाति।"  
> "Wherever a planet casts its sight, there it gives its results."

Aspects are crucial for understanding:
- How planets modify each other's effects
- Hidden influences on houses
- Timing of events through transits
- Strength and activation of [[Yogas]]
- Overall chart synthesis

The classical texts detailing aspect principles include:
- **Brihat Parashara Hora Shastra** (BPHS)
- **Brihat Jataka** by Varahamihira
- **Saravali** by Kalyana Varma
- **Jataka Parijata** by Vaidyanatha Dikshita

## Full Aspects vs. Partial Aspects

Vedic astrology recognizes different aspect strengths:

| Aspect Type | Strength | Description |
|-------------|----------|-------------|
| **Purna Drishti** (पूर्ण दृष्टि) | Full (100%) | Complete aspect, full influence |
| **Three-Quarter Aspect** | 75% | Strong but not complete |
| **Half Aspect** | 50% | Moderate influence |
| **Quarter Aspect** | 25% | Weak influence |

## General Aspect Rules (All Planets)

### The 7th House Aspect (Universal)

**Rule:** Every planet aspects the 7th house from its position with **full aspect** (100%).

**Example:**
- Planet in 1st house → aspects 7th house
- Planet in 3rd house → aspects 9th house
- Planet in 12th house → aspects 6th house

**Rationale:** 
- 7th house is directly opposite (180°)
- Represents completion and balance
- Universal law of opposition

**Interpretation:**
- Creates mutual influence
- Can support or challenge depending on planetary nature
- Modifies house significations significantly

## Special Planetary Aspects

Beyond the universal 7th house aspect, certain planets have **special aspects** based on their nature and traditional associations.

### Mars (Mangala) - Warrior's Aspects

**Special Aspects:**
1. **4th house aspect** (75% strength)
2. **7th house aspect** (100% - universal)
3. **8th house aspect** (100% - full special aspect)

**Visual Representation:**
```
If Mars in 1st house:
- Aspects 4th house (75%)
- Aspects 7th house (100%)
- Aspects 8th house (100%)
```

**Rationale:**
- **4th aspect:** Land, property, mother (Mars conquers territory)
- **8th aspect:** Sudden events, aggression, battles (Mars brings danger/transformation)

**Effects:**
- **4th house aspect:** Affects property, home environment, mother's health, vehicles
  - Positive: Ownership of land, protective home, strong mother
  - Negative: Property disputes, home conflicts, mother's health issues
  
- **8th house aspect:** Influences longevity, inheritance, sudden changes
  - Positive: Courage in crisis, surgical healing, research abilities
  - Negative: Accidents, sudden losses, chronic diseases

**Example Applications:**
- **Mars in 1st aspecting 4th:** Strong property ownership, assertive at home
- **Mars in 5th aspecting 8th:** Children involved in research/occult/danger
- **Mars in 10th aspecting 1st:** Aggressive personality, muscular body

### Jupiter (Guru) - Benevolent Aspects

**Special Aspects:**
1. **5th house aspect** (100% - full)
2. **7th house aspect** (100% - universal)
3. **9th house aspect** (100% - full)

**Visual Representation:**
```
If Jupiter in 1st house:
- Aspects 5th house (100%)
- Aspects 7th house (100%)
- Aspects 9th house (100%)
```

**Rationale:**
- **5th aspect:** Wisdom, children, intellect (Jupiter as teacher blesses progeny)
- **9th aspect:** Dharma, fortune, higher knowledge (Jupiter's natural house)

**Effects:**
- **5th house aspect:** Blesses children, education, creativity, intelligence
  - Positive: Wise children, good education, creative intelligence
  - Negative: (Rarely negative - may create overconfidence)
  
- **9th house aspect:** Enhances fortune, father's well-being, spirituality
  - Positive: Good luck, spiritual inclination, higher education, foreign travels
  - Negative: (Minimal - may create dogmatic beliefs)

**Special Importance:** Jupiter's aspects are considered **highly auspicious** and can protect/strengthen houses even from difficult positions.

**Example Applications:**
- **Jupiter in 3rd aspecting 5th, 9th, 11th:** Communication leads to education, fortune, gains
- **Jupiter in 6th aspecting 10th, 12th, 2nd:** Service leads to career success, spiritual expenses, family wealth
- **Jupiter in 12th aspecting 4th, 8th, 10th:** Foreign residence brings property, transformation, career success

**Protective Power:** 
> "गुरोर्दृष्टि सर्वाशुभनाशनं।"  
> "Jupiter's aspect destroys all inauspiciousness."

### Saturn (Shani) - Restrictive Aspects

**Special Aspects:**
1. **3rd house aspect** (100% - full)
2. **7th house aspect** (100% - universal)
3. **10th house aspect** (100% - full)

**Visual Representation:**
```
If Saturn in 1st house:
- Aspects 3rd house (100%)
- Aspects 7th house (100%)
- Aspects 10th house (100%)
```

**Rationale:**
- **3rd aspect:** Effort, courage, siblings (Saturn demands hard work in efforts)
- **10th aspect:** Career, authority, status (Saturn is the karaka of profession)

**Effects:**
- **3rd house aspect:** Affects siblings, courage, communication, short travels
  - Positive: Persistent efforts, disciplined siblings, serious communication
  - Negative: Obstacles through siblings, fearfulness, communication blocks
  
- **10th house aspect:** Influences career, reputation, public image
  - Positive: Steady career growth, authority through discipline, lasting reputation
  - Negative: Career delays, struggles with authority, public criticism

**Important Note:** Saturn's aspects bring **delay, discipline, and lasting results** but also **restriction and challenges**.

**Example Applications:**
- **Saturn in 1st aspecting 3rd, 7th, 10th:** Serious personality affecting efforts, marriage, and career—delayed but stable
- **Saturn in 4th aspecting 6th, 10th, 1st:** Property issues affecting service, career delays, restricted personality
- **Saturn in 7th aspecting 9th, 1st, 4th:** Delayed marriage affecting fortune, personality maturity, property acquisition later in life

**Saturn's Sade Sati Connection:**
When Saturn transits 12th, 1st, and 2nd from natal Moon, it aspects:
- From 12th → aspects 2nd (family), 6th (health), 9th (fortune)
- From 1st → aspects 3rd (efforts), 7th (marriage), 10th (career)
- From 2nd → aspects 4th (home), 8th (longevity), 11th (gains)

All areas of life get touched by Saturn's restricting influence.

### Rahu and Ketu - Nodal Aspects

**Traditional View:** Nodes don't have special aspects beyond the 7th house

**Modern/Practical View:** Some schools give them aspects similar to Saturn or Mars, but this is **not classical**

**7th House Aspect:**
- Both Rahu and Ketu aspect 7th house from their position (100%)
- Creates confusion, illusion, or sudden changes in aspected house

**Interpretation:**
- **Rahu's aspect:** Brings obsession, worldly desires, foreign influences, illusions
- **Ketu's aspect:** Brings detachment, spiritual inclination, sudden losses, liberation

**Example:**
- **Rahu in 1st aspecting 7th:** Obsession with relationships, foreign spouse, unusual marriage
- **Ketu in 4th aspecting 10th:** Detachment from career, spiritual profession, unconventional work

### Sun, Moon, Mercury, Venus - No Special Aspects

These planets only have the **universal 7th house aspect** (100%).

**Sun (Surya):**
- Aspects 7th house only
- Brings authority, ego, leadership to aspected house
- Can combust planets in close conjunction (within 3-10°)

**Moon (Chandra):**
- Aspects 7th house only
- Brings emotional influence, fluctuation, nurturing
- Waxing Moon aspects are beneficial, waning Moon less so

**Mercury (Budha):**
- Aspects 7th house only
- Brings intelligence, communication, commercial skills
- Aspects are neutral (benefic when with benefics, malefic when with malefics)

**Venus (Shukra):**
- Aspects 7th house only
- Brings beauty, luxury, harmony, relationships
- Highly beneficial aspect (natural benefic)

## Aspect Strength Calculation

### Distance-Based Strength

While house-based aspects are primary, some systems consider **degree-based strength**:

| Aspect Type | Angle | Strength |
|-------------|-------|----------|
| **Opposition** | 180° (±10°) | Full strength |
| **Trine** | 120° (±8°) | Strong (though not emphasized in Vedic) |
| **Square** | 90° (±8°) | Moderate (considered in some schools) |
| **Exact degree** | 0° (same degree in opposite house) | Maximum strength |

**Traditional Vedic Focus:** House-based aspects matter more than degree-based, unlike Western astrology.

### Rashi Drishti (Sign Aspect)

Some classical texts mention specific sign-to-sign aspects:

**Kendra Aspects (Angular):**
- Signs 1-4-7-10 from each other have mutual "seeing"
- Considered supportive structure

**Trikona Aspects (Trinal):**
- Signs 1-5-9 from each other have harmonious "sight"
- Highly beneficial connections

**Practical Usage:** Less commonly used than house-based aspects in modern practice

## Benefic vs. Malefic Aspects

### Benefic Planet Aspects

**Jupiter and Venus:**
- Aspects **protect, enhance, and bless**
- Even from dusthana houses (6, 8, 12), their aspects can improve conditions
- Called "**Shubha Drishti**" (auspicious sight)

**Effects:**
- Increase house prosperity
- Protect from afflictions
- Bring wisdom, growth, harmony
- Reduce malefic influences

**Mercury (conditional benefic):**
- With benefics → beneficial aspects
- With malefics → neutral or slightly malefic aspects

**Moon (when bright):**
- Waxing Moon (Shukla Paksha) aspects are beneficial
- Full Moon aspects highly auspicious
- Nurturing, emotional, protective influence

### Malefic Planet Aspects

**Saturn, Mars, Sun:**
- Aspects **restrict, challenge, and test**
- Can cause delays, obstacles, conflicts
- Called "**Papa Drishti**" (inauspicious sight)

**Effects:**
- Create obstacles and delays
- Bring discipline through hardship
- Test strength and resilience
- Can damage house significations if too strong

**Rahu and Ketu:**
- Confuse, create illusions, sudden changes
- Unconventional, karmic implications
- Neither purely benefic nor malefic—depends on placement

### Waxing vs. Waning Moon

**Shukla Paksha Moon (Waxing):**
- From New Moon to Full Moon
- Increasingly beneficial
- Nourishing aspects

**Krishna Paksha Moon (Waning):**
- From Full Moon to New Moon
- Decreasingly beneficial (more malefic)
- Depleting aspects

## Mutual Aspects (Paraspara Drishti)

When two planets aspect each other from 7th house positions, they create **mutual aspect** or **exchange of influence**.

### Example: Planet in 1st and Planet in 7th

Both aspect each other completely (100% each way).

**Effects:**
- **Strong mutual modification** of both planets
- **Combined results** in respective houses
- **Relationship dynamics** if personal planets involved

**Benefic-Benefic Mutual Aspect:**
- Jupiter in 1st, Venus in 7th → Wisdom and beauty enhance each other
- Harmonious, supportive, prosperous

**Malefic-Malefic Mutual Aspect:**
- Mars in 1st, Saturn in 7th → Conflict between aggression and restriction
- Challenging, creates tension, delays with anger

**Benefic-Malefic Mutual Aspect:**
- Jupiter in 1st, Mars in 7th → Wisdom tempers aggression
- Mixed results, one moderates the other
- Can create balance or conflict

**Special Case: Rahu-Ketu Axis:**
- Always 180° apart (6th and 7th house from each other)
- Always in mutual aspect
- Creates karmic axis affecting both areas

## Aspects in Yogas

Aspects play crucial role in [[Yogas]] formation and strength.

### Raja Yoga Through Aspect

**Formation:** Kendra lord and Trikona lord **mutually aspecting** each other

**Example:**
- Lord of 5th in 3rd house
- Lord of 10th in 9th house
- They aspect each other (3rd-9th are 7 houses apart)
- Creates Raja Yoga through aspect connection

**Strength:** Slightly weaker than conjunction or placement in each other's houses, but still effective.

### Neecha Bhanga Through Aspect

Debilitated planet's weakness cancelled when:
- Aspected by its **dispositor** (lord of sign it's placed in)
- Aspected by **exaltation lord**

**Example:**
- Sun debilitated in Libra (7th house)
- Venus (dispositor) aspects Sun from 1st house
- Neecha Bhanga occurs

### Yoga Bhanga (Breaking) Through Aspect

Strong malefic aspects can **break or weaken** yogas:

**Example:**
- 5th and 9th lords conjoined in 5th house (strong Raja Yoga)
- Saturn aspects this conjunction
- Yoga weakened → delays, obstacles in manifestation

## Aspect on Houses

### Analyzing House Strength

**Strong House Indicators:**
1. Benefics placed in house
2. Benefics aspecting house
3. House lord strong and well-placed
4. No malefic aspects

**Weak/Afflicted House Indicators:**
1. Malefics placed in house
2. Multiple malefic aspects
3. House lord weak or in dusthana
4. No benefic support

### Example: 10th House (Career) Analysis

**Strong 10th House:**
- Jupiter in 10th house (benefic placement) ✓
- Venus aspects 10th from 4th (benefic aspect) ✓
- 10th lord in Kendra (strong lord) ✓
- **Result:** Excellent career, recognition, prosperity

**Afflicted 10th House:**
- 10th house empty
- Mars aspects 10th from 1st (malefic aspect) ✗
- Saturn aspects 10th from 7th (malefic aspect) ✗
- 10th lord in 8th house (weak lord) ✗
- **Result:** Career struggles, obstacles, conflicts with authority

### Cancellation of Afflictions

**Jupiter's Protective Aspect:**
- Can reduce harm from malefic aspects
- Doesn't completely remove but provides resilience

**Example:**
- 7th house aspected by Mars (conflict in marriage)
- 7th house also aspected by Jupiter (protection, wisdom)
- **Result:** Initial marital conflicts but eventual harmony through wisdom

## Aspect on Planets

### Planetary Strength Through Aspects

**Benefic Aspects on Planet:**
- Enhance planet's positive significations
- Provide support and protection
- Increase confidence and results

**Malefic Aspects on Planet:**
- Challenge planet's significations
- Create obstacles and tests
- Can weaken or delay results

### Combustion vs. Aspect

**Combustion (Close Conjunction with Sun):**
- Planet within 3-10° of Sun (varies by planet)
- Weakens planet severely
- Different from aspect

**Sun's Aspect:**
- 7th house aspect from Sun
- Brings authority, ego, but NOT combustion
- Planet is influenced but not burned

### Example: Moon Under Aspects

**Moon with Benefic Aspects:**
- Jupiter aspect → Emotional wisdom, optimism
- Venus aspect → Emotional harmony, beauty
- **Result:** Stable mind, happiness

**Moon with Malefic Aspects:**
- Saturn aspect → Emotional restriction, depression
- Mars aspect → Anger, emotional volatility
- Rahu aspect → Confusion, anxiety
- **Result:** Mental challenges, mood disorders

**Moon with Mixed Aspects:**
- Jupiter and Saturn both aspect Moon
- Wisdom (Jupiter) battles restriction (Saturn)
- **Result:** Maturity through emotional challenges

## Aspect in [[Divisional Charts]]

Aspects work the same way in divisional charts as in Rasi chart (D-1).

**D-9 (Navamsa) Aspects:**
- Especially important for marriage (7th house aspects)
- Jupiter's aspect in D-9 protects marriage
- Mars/Saturn aspects in D-9 create marital challenges

**D-10 (Dasamsa) Aspects:**
- Important for career
- 10th house aspects indicate professional support/obstacles

**General Rule:** Aspects in divisional charts show **specific area influences** in more detail

## Transits and Aspects (Gocharas)

Transiting planets' aspects activate natal chart points.

### Double Transit Theory (Vedha)

**Important Principle:**
When both **Jupiter and Saturn** simultaneously aspect or occupy a house from **natal Moon**, that house gets **strongly activated**.

**Example:**
- Natal Moon in Cancer
- Transit Jupiter in Scorpio (aspects Cancer - 5th aspect forward becomes 9th back)
- Transit Saturn in Capricorn (aspects Cancer - 7th aspect)
- Both aspect natal Moon → Major life changes related to 1st house (self, body, personality)

**Applications:**
- Marriage often happens during double transit on 7th house from Moon
- Career changes during double transit on 10th house from Moon
- Child birth during double transit on 5th house from Moon

### Malefic Transit Aspects

**Saturn's Transit Aspect:**
- Brings tests, delays, maturity
- 7.5-year Sade Sati aspects many houses

**Mars Transit Aspect:**
- Short duration (1.5 months per sign)
- Brings energy or conflicts temporarily

**Rahu/Ketu Transit Aspects:**
- Create sudden events, illusions
- Karmic activations

### Benefic Transit Aspects

**Jupiter's Transit Aspect:**
- Protection and growth (1 year per sign)
- Highly beneficial timing for aspected house matters

**Venus Transit Aspect:**
- Short duration (1 month per sign)
- Brings temporary pleasure, harmony

More details on transits in [[Transits (Gocharas)]].

## Practical Applications

### Analyzing Relationship Compatibility

**7th House from Both Charts:**
- Check mutual aspects between Venus, Mars, Moon
- Jupiter's aspect on partner's Venus/7th lord → protection
- Saturn's aspect on partner's Venus/7th lord → delays, tests

See [[Marriage Compatibility]] for detailed methodology.

### Career Timing

**10th House Activation:**
- Saturn's aspect on natal 10th → career restructuring
- Jupiter's aspect on natal 10th → career expansion
- Mars aspect on natal 10th → career action, competition

### Dasha Interpretation

**During Planet's Dasha:**
- Houses aspected by dasha lord get activated
- Benefic aspects → positive events in those areas
- Malefic aspects → challenges in those areas

**Example: Jupiter Dasha**
- Jupiter in 3rd house aspects 7th, 9th, 11th
- During Jupiter dasha: marriage (7th), fortune (9th), gains (11th) emphasized

## Special Considerations

### Retrograde Planets' Aspects

**Traditional View:** Retrograde planets aspect houses the same way

**Practical Interpretation:**
- Aspect strength may be internalized or delayed
- Effects manifest differently (more karmic, revisiting past themes)
- Not necessarily weaker, but different quality

### Combust Planets' Aspects

**Combust Planet:**
- Can still aspect other houses/planets
- But its own strength is weakened
- Aspect is "colored" by Sun's influence

### Aspect from Dusthana Houses (6, 8, 12)

**General Rule:** Aspect from dusthana houses can bring challenges even from benefics

**Example:**
- Jupiter in 8th house aspects 12th, 2nd, 4th
- While beneficial, may bring: spiritual expenses (12th), family complexities (2nd), property changes (4th)

**Malefics in Dusthana:**
- Can actually help (Vipreet Raja Yoga principle)
- Saturn in 8th aspecting 10th → Career through research, occult, or transformation

## Synthesis with Chart Reading

### Step-by-Step Aspect Analysis

1. **Identify Planet Position**
   - Note which house planet occupies
   - Check planet's dignity and strength

2. **Determine Aspects Given**
   - 7th house aspect (all planets)
   - Special aspects (Mars, Jupiter, Saturn)

3. **Analyze Houses Aspected**
   - House significations
   - House lord and its position
   - Other planets in aspected house

4. **Assess Aspect Quality**
   - Benefic or malefic planet?
   - Functional benefic/malefic for ascendant?
   - Yoga formation through aspects?

5. **Check Aspects Received**
   - Which planets aspect the planet in question?
   - Supporting or challenging aspects?

6. **Synthesize Total Picture**
   - Combine placement + aspects given + aspects received
   - Consider in context of [[Planetary Periods (Dashas)]]
   - Check confirmation in [[Divisional Charts]]

### Common Patterns

**Planet Receiving No Aspects:**
- Operates independently
- Can be very powerful (unmodified energy)
- Or isolated (no support)

**Planet Receiving Multiple Benefic Aspects:**
- Highly supported and protected
- Strong positive results

**Planet Receiving Multiple Malefic Aspects:**
- Heavily challenged
- Requires remedies (see [[Remedies]])
- Results delayed or blocked

**Planet Receiving Mixed Aspects:**
- Complex results
- Good and bad experiences
- Depends on dasha timing

## Important Principles to Remember

1. **House-Based System:** Vedic aspects are house-to-house, not degree-based primarily

2. **Special Aspects Matter:** Mars (4,7,8), Jupiter (5,7,9), Saturn (3,7,10) have extra influence

3. **Benefic vs. Malefic:** Quality of aspecting planet determines effect nature

4. **Context is Key:** Same aspect can help or harm depending on overall chart

5. **Yogas Through Aspects:** Many important [[Yogas]] form through aspect connections

6. **Transit Activation:** Aspects from transiting planets activate natal chart

7. **Divisional Confirmation:** Check aspects in D-9 for sustained effects

8. **Remedies Help:** Malefic aspects can be mitigated through [[Remedies]]

## Conclusion

Aspects (Drishti) are fundamental to Vedic astrological analysis, creating the web of influences that makes each chart unique. Understanding aspects allows the astrologer to:

- See hidden influences on houses and planets
- Predict timing of events through transits
- Understand relationship dynamics
- Assess yoga strength and activation
- Recommend targeted remedies

**Ancient Wisdom:**
> "दृष्टिर्बलवती सर्वत्र।"  
> "Aspect is powerful everywhere."

The interplay of planetary placements and aspects creates the complex tapestry of karma, and understanding this interplay is essential for accurate prediction and effective remediation.
