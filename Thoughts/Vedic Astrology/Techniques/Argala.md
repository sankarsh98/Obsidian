# Argala (Planetary Intervention)

## Introduction

**Argala** (अरगला) literally means a "Bolt" or "Latch" (like on a door). In Vedic Astrology, it refers to the **Intervention** of a planet upon a House or another Planet.

*   **Primary Function:** It shows how the affairs of one house are influenced, supported, or obstructed by the affairs of another house.
*   **Usage:** Crucial in **Jaimini Astrology** (Char Dasha) but also applicable in Parashari.

## The Concept

Every house has specific houses that act as its "interventionists" (Argala) and specific houses that "remove the intervention" (Virodha Argala).

### Primary Argala Positions

For any House or Planet, Argala is caused by planets placed in:
1.  **4th House:** Primary Support (Sukh Argala).
2.  **2nd House:** Monetary/Family Support (Dhan Argala).
3.  **11th House:** Gain Support (Labh Argala).
4.  **5th House:** Secondary Support (only for Ketu).

### Obstruction (Virodha Argala)

For every "Bolt" (Argala), there is an "Unlatching" position (Virodha) that cancels the effect.

| Argala Position | Virodha (Obstruction) Position |
|-----------------|--------------------------------|
| **4th House** | **10th House** |
| **2nd House** | **12th House** |
| **11th House** | **3rd House** |
| **5th House** (Ketu) | **9th House** |

**Rule of Strength:**
*   If the Argala planet is stronger than the Virodha planet, the Argala (Influence) prevails.
*   If the Virodha planet is stronger, the Intervention is blocked.

## Practical Interpretation

### Example: Argala on the Lagna (Self)

1.  **Planet in 4th House (Argala):**
    *   *Effect:* The person is influenced by their mother, home environment, or happiness.
    *   *Blocker:* If a stronger planet is in the 10th (Career), work demands block the home influence.

2.  **Planet in 2nd House (Argala):**
    *   *Effect:* Family and wealth sustain the person.
    *   *Blocker:* Planet in 12th (Losses) removes this support.

3.  **Planet in 11th House (Argala):**
    *   *Effect:* Friends and gains support the person.
    *   *Blocker:* Planet in 3rd (Effort/Siblings) blocks this (i.e., you have to work hard yourself rather than relying on gains).

### Shubha vs. Papa Argala

*   **Shubha Argala (Benefic Intervention):**
    *   Caused by benefics (Jupiter, Venus, etc.).
    *   Result: Prosperity, easy success, help comes naturally.
*   **Papa Argala (Malefic Intervention):**
    *   Caused by malefics (Saturn, Rahu, etc.).
    *   Result: The person is "bolted in" by difficult circumstances, but if the planet is strong, it can force success through pressure.

## Argala in Career (10th House)

*   Planets in the **1st** (4th from 10th), **11th** (2nd from 10th), and **8th** (11th from 10th) cause Argala on Career.
*   *Interpretation:* Your Career is supported by:
    *   **Yourself (1st):** Personal reputation.
    *   **Gains (11th):** Professional network.
    *   **Change/Research (8th):** Hidden knowledge or transformation.
