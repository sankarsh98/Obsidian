# Marriage Compatibility (Kundali Milan)

## Introduction

In Vedic Astrology, **Marriage Compatibility** or **Kundali Milan** (Matching of Charts) is a critical step before finalizing a union. It is not merely about romance but about ensuring longevity, prosperity, progeny, and mental harmony between the couple.

The system evaluates the compatibility primarily based on the **Moon Signs** (Janma Rashi) and **Nakshatras** of the bride and groom.

## The Ashta Koota System (The 36 Points)

The most widely used method is the **Ashta Koota** (Eight Categories) system, which assigns a total score of **36 Gunas** (Points).

| Koota (Category) | Max Points | Area of Life Influenced |
|------------------|------------|-------------------------|
| **1. Varna** | 1 | Ego / Work compatibility / Spiritual grade |
| **2. Vashya** | 2 | Attraction / Control / Power dynamics |
| **3. Tara** | 3 | Destiny / Luck / Health compatibility |
| **4. Yoni** | 4 | Sexual compatibility / Biological instincts |
| **5. Graha Maitri**| 5 | Psychological friendship / Mental harmony |
| **6. Gana** | 6 | Temperament (Divine/Human/Demon) |
| **7. Bhakoot** | 7 | Emotional welfare / Family growth / Happiness |
| **8. Nadi** | 8 | Health / Genetics / Progeny (Most Critical) |
| **TOTAL** | **36** | |

### Interpretation of Scores

*   **< 18 Points:** **Not Recommended.** High potential for conflict, separation, or unhappiness.
*   **18 - 24 Points:** **Average.** Acceptable, but requires effort and remedies.
*   **25 - 32 Points:** **Very Good.** Strong compatibility and happiness.
*   **33 - 36 Points:** **Excellent.** Rare and extremely auspicious (often considered divine).

### Detailed Breakdown of Kootas

#### 1. Varna (Caste/Spirit) - 1 Pt
Classifies signs into Brahmin (Water), Kshatriya (Fire), Vaishya (Earth), Shudra (Air).
*   *Purpose:* Checks if spiritual/ego levels align. Ideally, the groom's capacity should be equal to or higher than the bride's to maintain traditional harmony (though less relevant in modern times).

#### 2. Vashya (Control) - 2 Pts
Classifies signs into Human, Lion, Quadruped, Water-dweller, Insect.
*   *Purpose:* Determines who will influence whom. Checks for natural enmity (e.g., Lion eats Human).

#### 3. Tara (Star) - 3 Pts
Compares the distance between birth [[Nakshatras]].
*   *Purpose:* Ensures luck and longevity. Calculated by counting from Bride to Groom and Groom to Bride.

#### 4. Yoni (Nature) - 4 Pts
Assigns an animal symbol to each Nakshatra (e.g., Horse, Elephant, Serpent, Cat).
*   *Purpose:* **Sexual Compatibility**.
    *   Same animal = Perfect match (4 pts).
    *   Friendly animals = Good.
    *   Enemy animals (e.g., Cat vs. Rat, Snake vs. Mongoose) = 0 pts. Indicates sexual frustration or repulsion.

#### 5. Graha Maitri (Planetary Friendship) - 5 Pts
Compares the lords of the Moon Signs (Rashi Lords).
*   *Purpose:* **Mental Compatibility**.
    *   If Moon lords are friends (e.g., Sun and Jupiter) = Harmony.
    *   If enemies (e.g., Sun and Saturn) = Clash of personalities/ego.

#### 6. Gana (Temperament) - 6 Pts
Classifies Nakshatras into three Ganas:
1.  **Deva (Divine):** Gentle, charitable (e.g., Ashwini, Pushya).
2.  **Manushya (Human):** Practical, social (e.g., Bharani, Rohini).
3.  **Rakshasa (Demon):** Intense, aggressive, strong-willed (e.g., Krittika, Ashlesha).
*   *Purpose:* Lifestyle matching.
    *   Deva + Deva = Perfect.
    *   Deva + Rakshasa = Conflict (0 pts) - Sensitive vs. Aggressive.

#### 7. Bhakoot (Relative Position) - 7 Pts
Checks the position of the Groom's Moon relative to the Bride's Moon.
*   **Auspicous:** 1/1, 3/11, 4/10 positions.
*   **Inauspicious (Dosha):**
    *   **6/8 (Shadashtaka):** Fatal to love. High conflict/death.
    *   **2/12 (Dwirdwadasha):** Poverty/Expense issues.
    *   **9/5 (Navam-Pancham):** Ego/Progeny issues (sometimes acceptable).

#### 8. Nadi (Pulse/Genetics) - 8 Pts
Classifies Nakshatras into Vata, Pitta, Kapha (Adi, Madhya, Antya).
*   *Purpose:* **Health and Progeny**.
    *   Rule: Bride and Groom should **NOT** have the same Nadi.
    *   Same Nadi = **Nadi Dosha** (0 pts). Indicates genetic incompatibility, weak children, or infertility.

## Mangal Dosha (Mars Blemish)

Even with a high point score, a match may be rejected due to **Mangal Dosha** (Kuya Dosha).

**Definition:** Mars (Mangal) placed in the 1st, 2nd, 4th, 7th, 8th, or 12th house in the [[Birth Chart (Kundali)]].
*   *Effect:* Mars is a planet of war. In these houses, it attacks marriage factors (Spouse's life, domestic peace, sexual harmony).

**Exceptions (Cancellation):**
*   If both partners have Mangal Dosha, it cancels out.
*   Mars in specific signs (e.g., Aries, Scorpio, Capricorn) often cancels the Dosha.
*   If Mars is aspected by Jupiter.

## Beyond the Points: The "Manual" Check

An experienced astrologer never relies solely on the computer-generated score (Gun Milan). They must check:
1.  **Longevity (Ayu):** Do both charts promise a long life? (No point matching ghosts).
2.  **7th House Condition:** Is the 7th house/lord afflicted in the individual charts? (e.g., propensity for divorce).
3.  **Dasha Sandhi:** Are both running difficult Dashas simultaneously?
4.  **Putra Yoga:** Promise of children.

## Conclusion

A score of **18+ is the minimum**, but the presence of **Nadi Dosha** or **Bhakoot Dosha** (0 points in #7 or #8) is often a dealbreaker unless specific cancellations exist. **Graha Maitri** (Mental friendship) is crucial for modern relationships where conversation and understanding matter more than traditional roles.
