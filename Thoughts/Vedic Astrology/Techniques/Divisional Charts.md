# Divisional Charts (Varga Charts)

## Introduction

**Divisional Charts**, known as **Varga Charts** (वर्ग) or **Amshas** (अंश), are a unique and sophisticated feature of Vedic Astrology that provides microscopic examination of specific life areas. While the [[Birth Chart (Kundali)]] (Rasi Chart or D-1) shows the overall life picture, divisional charts zoom into particular domains like marriage, career, children, spiritual life, and more.

The system of divisional charts is mentioned extensively in classical texts like:
- **Brihat Parashara Hora Shastra** (BPHS) - Primary source
- **Jataka Parijata** by Vaidyanatha Dikshita
- **Brihat Jataka** by Varahamihira
- **Uttara Kalamrita** by Kalidasa

Sage Parashara described **16 primary divisional charts** (Shodasha Vargas), though many more exist in advanced practice.

## Philosophy and Purpose

### Why Divisional Charts?

**Core Principle:** A sign in the birth chart spans 30 degrees. Two people born in the same city within 2 hours might have identical Rasi charts, yet completely different lives. Divisional charts provide the fine-tuning that distinguishes these charts.

**Analogy:** 
- **Rasi Chart (D-1)** = Viewing a country from satellite
- **Divisional Charts** = Zooming into specific cities, neighborhoods, houses

### How Division Works

Each sign (30°) is mathematically divided into smaller segments:
- **D-2 (Hora):** 2 divisions of 15° each
- **D-3 (Drekkana):** 3 divisions of 10° each
- **D-9 (Navamsa):** 9 divisions of 3°20' each
- **D-10 (Dasamsa):** 10 divisions of 3° each
- And so on...

**Important:** Divisional chart positions depend on the **exact degree** of planets in the Rasi chart. Even a few minutes of arc can change divisional positions.

## Most Important Divisional Charts

### D-1: Rasi Chart (Birth Chart)

**Purpose:** Foundation chart showing overall life blueprint

**Significations:**
- Physical body and health
- Personality and character
- General life trends
- All 12 houses and their basic significations

**Usage:** 
- Starting point for all analysis
- Must be strong for divisional charts to fully manifest
- Shows the "what" of life events

**Key Principle:** "A weak D-1 cannot give strong results even if divisional charts are excellent."

### D-2: Hora Chart (होरा)

**Division Method:** Each sign divided into 2 parts of 15° each

**Calculation:**
- **Odd signs** (Aries, Gemini, Leo, Libra, Sagittarius, Aquarius):
  - 0° to 15° → Sun's Hora → Goes to Leo
  - 15° to 30° → Moon's Hora → Goes to Cancer
- **Even signs** (Taurus, Cancer, Virgo, Scorpio, Capricorn, Pisces):
  - 0° to 15° → Moon's Hora → Goes to Cancer
  - 15° to 30° → Sun's Hora → Goes to Leo

**Result:** In D-2, all planets are either in Cancer or Leo

**Significations:**
- **Wealth** and financial prosperity
- **Money** accumulation capacity
- **Material resources**
- **Assets** and savings

**Interpretation:**

| Planet in D-2 | Meaning |
|---------------|---------|
| **Leo (Sun's Hora)** | Wealth through authority, government, father, personal efforts, independence |
| **Cancer (Moon's Hora)** | Wealth through mother, emotions, public, nurturing professions, fluctuating income |

**Important Factors:**
- Benefics (Jupiter, Venus, Mercury) in D-2 → Good wealth
- 2nd and 11th lords of D-1 strong in D-2 → Excellent finances
- Malefics (Saturn, Mars) in D-2 → Struggles with wealth, but Saturn can give gains through hard work

### D-3: Drekkana Chart (द्रेष्काण)

**Division Method:** Each sign divided into 3 parts of 10° each

**Calculation (Parashara System):**
- **First Drekkana** (0° to 10°): Same sign
- **Second Drekkana** (10° to 20°): 5th sign from current sign
- **Third Drekkana** (20° to 30°): 9th sign from current sign

**Example:** Planet at 25° Aries
- Falls in 3rd Drekkana (20° to 30°)
- Goes to 9th sign from Aries = Sagittarius in D-3

**Significations:**
- **Siblings** (brothers and sisters)
- **Courage** and valor
- **Efforts** and initiatives
- **Communication** within family
- **Younger relatives**

**Interpretation:**
- 3rd house and 3rd lord in D-3 → Sibling relationships
- Malefics in 3rd house of D-3 → Conflicts with siblings
- Benefics in 3rd house of D-3 → Supportive siblings
- Strong Mars in D-3 → Courage, competitive nature

**Special Use:** Analyzing detailed effects of 3rd house matters from the Rasi chart

### D-9: Navamsa Chart (नवांश) - MOST IMPORTANT

The Navamsa is considered the **second most important chart** after the Rasi chart and is essential for marriage, dharma, and spiritual analysis.

**Division Method:** Each sign divided into 9 parts of 3°20' each

**Calculation:**
- **Aries, Leo, Sagittarius** (Fire signs): Navamsas from Aries to Sagittarius
- **Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn** (Earth signs): Navamsas from Capricorn to Virgo
- **Gemini, Libra, Aquarius** (Air signs): Navamsas from Libra to Gemini
- **Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces** (Water signs): Navamsas from Cancer to Pisces

**Formula:**
```
Navamsa = [(Planet's degree in sign × 9) / 30] + Starting Navamsa of that sign
```

**Example:** Sun at 15°30' in Taurus
- 15.5° × 9 = 139.5
- 139.5 / 30 = 4.65 → 5th division
- Taurus starts from Capricorn, so 5th division = Taurus
- Sun in Taurus in D-9

**Primary Significations:**
1. **Marriage and Spouse** - Most important use
2. **Dharma** (Life purpose, spiritual path)
3. **Inner self** and soul evolution
4. **Second half of life** (after age 30-35)
5. **Strength of planets** (dignity in D-9 crucial)

**Key Principles:**

**1. Spouse Analysis:**
- **7th house in D-9:** Spouse's nature, appearance, character
- **7th lord in D-9:** Spouse's quality, marital happiness
- **Venus in D-9** (for men): Wife's characteristics
- **Jupiter in D-9** (for women): Husband's characteristics
- **Upapada Lagna:** Special lagna for spouse in D-9

**2. Vargottama:**
- Planet in **same sign** in D-1 and D-9 = Vargottama (वर्गोत्तम)
- **Extremely powerful** - planet gains exceptional strength
- Results: Enhanced results, prominence of that planet's significations
- Example: Moon in Cancer in both D-1 and D-9 → Very strong emotional foundation

**3. Pushkara Navamsa:**
Certain Navamsas are considered especially auspicious:

| Sign | Pushkara Navamsas (Degrees) | Corresponding Sign in D-9 |
|------|---------------------------|---------------------------|
| Aries, Leo, Sagittarius | 21°40' to 23°20' | Sagittarius |
| Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn | 18°20' to 20° | Taurus |
| Gemini, Libra, Aquarius | 15° to 16°40' | Aquarius |
| Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces | 25° to 26°40' | Pisces |

**Results:** Planets in Pushkara Navamsa give auspicious, fortunate results

**4. Planetary Strength in D-9:**

| D-9 Placement | Effect |
|---------------|--------|
| **Exalted** | Strong, auspicious results especially in second half of life |
| **Own sign** | Stable, good results in planet's significations |
| **Friendly sign** | Supportive, positive outcomes |
| **Debilitated** | Weakens planet even if strong in D-1 |
| **Vargottama** | Exceptional strength regardless of dignity |

**5. Marriage Timing:**
- Typically activated during dasha of 7th lord of D-1 or D-9
- When Jupiter transits 7th house of D-9
- Strong Venus/Jupiter periods

**6. Navamsa for Chart Confirmation:**
- **Planets strong in both D-1 and D-9:** Will definitely give results
- **Strong in D-1 but weak in D-9:** Results in first half of life only
- **Weak in D-1 but strong in D-9:** Delayed results but eventual success
- **Weak in both:** Difficult to manifest positive results

**7. Spiritual Evolution:**
- 12th house in D-9 → Moksha, spiritual liberation
- 9th house in D-9 → Dharma, religious inclinations
- Jupiter, Ketu positions → Spiritual path indicators

**Navamsa Lagna:**
- As important as Rasi Lagna for D-9 analysis
- Shows inner personality, soul nature
- Planets in Kendra from Navamsa Lagna are very powerful

### D-10: Dasamsa Chart (दशांश)

**Division Method:** Each sign divided into 10 parts of 3° each

**Calculation:**
- **Odd signs:** Dasamsas from same sign, then next 9 signs in order
- **Even signs:** Dasamsas from 9th sign from it, then next 9 signs in order

**Example:** Planet at 5° in Aries (odd sign)
- 5° / 3° = 1.67 → 2nd division
- Starts from Aries: 2nd division = Taurus
- Planet in Taurus in D-10

**Significations:**
- **Career** and profession
- **Work environment**
- **Authority** and status
- **Public recognition**
- **Professional achievements**
- **Reputation** in society

**Key Analysis Points:**

**1. 10th House in D-10:**
- Shows specific career nature
- Sign indicates field of work
- Planets there indicate career activities

**2. 10th Lord of D-1 in D-10:**
- Its position shows career success level
- Exalted → High achievement
- Debilitated → Professional struggles

**3. Atmakaraka in D-10:**
- Shows soul's professional purpose
- Strong Atmakaraka in D-10 → Career fulfillment

**4. Profession Indicators:**

| Sign in 10th of D-10 | Career Field |
|----------------------|--------------|
| **Aries, Leo, Sagittarius** | Leadership, military, sports, entrepreneurship |
| **Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn** | Finance, administration, engineering, practical fields |
| **Gemini, Libra, Aquarius** | Communication, arts, social work, innovation |
| **Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces** | Healing, research, spirituality, psychology |

**5. Career Timing:**
- Strong career during dashas of planets well-placed in D-10
- 10th lord of D-10's period → Peak professional success

**6. Multiple Careers:**
- Multiple planets in 10th house of D-10 → Multiple income sources
- Fluctuating signs (Gemini, Virgo, Sagittarius, Pisces) → Career changes

### D-7: Saptamsa Chart (सप्तांश)

**Division Method:** Each sign divided into 7 parts of 4°17'8.57" each

**Calculation:**
- **Odd signs:** Start from same sign
- **Even signs:** Start from 7th sign from it
- Then count 7 divisions in order

**Significations:**
- **Children** (santana) - Most important use
- **Progeny** and fertility
- **Creativity** and creative output
- **5th house** matters in detail
- **Grandchildren**

**Key Analysis:**

**1. Children Possibility:**
- **5th house in D-7:** Nature of children
- **5th lord in D-7:** Children's well-being
- **Jupiter in D-7:** Male children (traditional)
- **Venus in D-7:** Female children (traditional)

**2. Number of Children:**
- Planets in 5th house of D-7 (count benefics mainly)
- 5th lord's position and aspects
- Fertility indicators (Moon, Venus, Jupiter positions)

**3. Children's Nature:**

| Planet in 5th of D-7 | Child's Nature |
|---------------------|----------------|
| **Sun** | Leadership, confident, health-focused |
| **Moon** | Emotional, caring, changeable |
| **Mars** | Energetic, athletic, competitive |
| **Mercury** | Intelligent, communicative, quick learner |
| **Jupiter** | Wise, spiritual, fortunate |
| **Venus** | Artistic, beautiful, luxury-loving |
| **Saturn** | Serious, disciplined, responsible (delayed) |

**4. Challenges with Children:**
- Malefics in 5th of D-7 → Difficulties
- 5th lord debilitated in D-7 → Issues conceiving or raising
- Rahu/Ketu in 5th of D-7 → Unusual situations, adoption possible

**5. Timing of Children:**
- Jupiter's transit over 5th house of D-7
- Dasha of 5th lord of D-1 or D-7
- Venus/Jupiter periods for women

### D-12: Dwadasamsa Chart (द्वादशांश)

**Division Method:** Each sign divided into 12 parts of 2°30' each

**Calculation:**
- Divisions start from same sign
- Each division corresponds to one sign of the zodiac in order

**Significations:**
- **Parents** (especially father and mother relationship)
- **Ancestors** and lineage
- **Past life karma** and inherited patterns
- **Parental legacy**
- **Family traditions**

**Key Analysis:**

**1. Parent Indicators:**
- **Sun in D-12:** Father's well-being and relationship
- **Moon in D-12:** Mother's well-being and relationship
- **9th house in D-12:** Father
- **4th house in D-12:** Mother

**2. Inherited Traits:**
- Strong planets in D-12 show inherited strengths
- Afflicted planets show inherited challenges
- Vargottama planets → Strong ancestral blessings

**3. Past Life Indications:**
- 12th house in D-12 → Past life patterns
- Saturn in D-12 → Karmic debts from ancestors
- Rahu/Ketu in D-12 → Unusual or foreign ancestry

### D-24: Chaturvimshamsa Chart (चतुर्विंशांश)

**Division Method:** Each sign divided into 24 parts of 1°15' each

**Calculation:**
- **Odd signs:** Start from Leo
- **Even signs:** Start from Cancer
- Repeat Leo-Cancer pattern 12 times

**Significations:**
- **Education** and learning (Vidya)
- **Academic achievements**
- **Knowledge acquisition**
- **Intellectual capabilities**
- **Degrees** and certifications

**Key Analysis:**

**1. Educational Success:**
- **4th house in D-24:** Early education
- **5th house in D-24:** Higher education, specialization
- **Mercury in D-24:** Learning ability
- **Jupiter in D-24:** Higher knowledge, philosophy

**2. Field of Study:**
- Planets in 4th/5th of D-24 indicate subjects
- Strong Mercury → Sciences, mathematics, communication
- Strong Venus → Arts, music, design
- Strong Mars → Engineering, sports, military studies

**3. Academic Interruptions:**
- Malefics in 4th/5th of D-24 → Educational obstacles
- 4th/5th lords weak → Difficulty completing education

### D-30: Trimshamsa Chart (त्रिंशांश)

**Division Method:** Each sign divided into 30 parts of 1° each

**Calculation (Unequal divisions):**
- Complex system with unequal portions given to different planets
- Varies between odd and even signs

**Significations:**
- **Evils** and misfortunes
- **Diseases** and afflictions
- **Enemies** and dangers
- **Accidents** and sudden events
- **Overall protection** level

**Usage:**
- Analyzing potential problems and their nature
- Understanding vulnerabilities
- Assessing protective factors
- NOT for routine analysis - used only for specific problem investigation

**Key Principle:** "Even malefics should be examined in D-30 for understanding the nature of difficulties."

### D-60: Shashtiamsa Chart (षष्टिअंश)

**Division Method:** Each sign divided into 60 parts of 0°30' (30 arc-minutes) each

**Significations:**
- **Summary of all previous lives** (Sanchita Karma)
- **Overall destiny** pattern
- **Inborn nature** from past karma
- **General fortune** or misfortune
- **Confirmation** of D-1 effects

**Importance:**
- Considered the **finest division**
- Requires **very accurate birth time** (within 1-2 minutes)
- Used by advanced astrologers for final confirmation
- Shows "why this soul has this particular chart"

**Usage:**
- Planet exalted in D-60 → Excellent past karma in that area
- Planet debilitated in D-60 → Past life debts in that area
- Vargottama in D-60 → Exceptionally strong karmic blessing

**Practical Challenge:** Requires birth time accuracy of ±1 minute, which is rarely available

## Working with Divisional Charts

### Vimshopaka Bala (विंशोपक बल)

**Definition:** A numerical scoring system assigning strength points to planets based on their dignity in various divisional charts.

**Traditional Vimshopaka (20-point scale):**

| Chart | Points | Weightage |
|-------|--------|-----------|
| D-1 (Rasi) | 3 | 15% |
| D-2 (Hora) | 1.5 | 7.5% |
| D-3 (Drekkana) | 1.5 | 7.5% |
| D-9 (Navamsa) | 3 | 15% |
| D-12 (Dwadasamsa) | 1.5 | 7.5% |
| D-30 (Trimshamsa) | 1.5 | 7.5% |
| **Total** | **12** | **60%** |

**Shad Varga (6 charts):** First 6 divisional charts

**Sapta Varga (7 charts):** Adds D-7 to above

**Dasha Varga (10 charts):** First 10 divisional charts

**Shodasha Varga (16 charts):** All 16 primary charts

**Scoring Method:**

| Position in Divisional Chart | Points |
|------------------------------|--------|
| **Exalted (Uccha)** | Full points |
| **Own Sign (Swa-kshetra)** | Full points |
| **Friendly Sign (Mitra)** | 3/4 points |
| **Neutral Sign (Sama)** | 1/2 points |
| **Enemy Sign (Shatru)** | 1/4 points |
| **Debilitated (Neecha)** | Zero points |

**Total Vimshopaka Strength Interpretation:**

| Total Score (out of 20) | Strength Level | Result |
|------------------------|----------------|--------|
| **18-20** | Parijatamsa (Extraordinary) | Exceptional results, fame, success |
| **15-18** | Uttamamsa (Excellent) | Very good results, prosperity |
| **12-15** | Gopuramsa (Good) | Good results, comfort |
| **8-12** | Simhasanamsa (Moderate) | Average results, some struggles |
| **5-8** | Paravatanamsa (Low) | Difficult results, obstacles |
| **Below 5** | Kimsamsaka (Very weak) | Poor results, severe difficulties |

**Practical Use:** Helps determine which planets will give best results during their dashas.

### Checking Planet Strength Across Charts

**Step-by-Step Method:**

1. **Analyze D-1 (Rasi) first**
   - Basic strength and dignity
   - House placement
   - Aspects

2. **Check D-9 (Navamsa) next**
   - Confirms D-1 strength
   - Shows second half of life
   - Marriage and dharma

3. **Examine relevant divisional chart**
   - D-2 for wealth questions
   - D-7 for children
   - D-10 for career
   - D-24 for education

4. **Calculate Vimshopaka Bala**
   - Overall strength across multiple charts
   - Dasha prediction reliability

5. **Look for Vargottama**
   - Same sign in D-1 and D-9 = exceptional power
   - Other vargottamas also strengthen

### Yoga Confirmation in Divisional Charts

**Important Principle:** Yogas in D-1 must be confirmed in divisional charts to fully manifest.

**Example:**
- **Raja Yoga in D-1:** 5th lord in 10th house
- **Check D-9:** Are these lords still connected?
- **Check D-10:** Is career potential confirmed?
- **If confirmed in multiple charts:** Yoga will definitely give results
- **If broken in D-9:** Results only until age 30-35, then fade

**Common Confirmations:**

| Yoga Type | Check in Charts | Why |
|-----------|-----------------|-----|
| **Dhana Yoga** | D-2, D-9 | Wealth confirmation, lasting prosperity |
| **Raja Yoga** | D-9, D-10 | Authority confirmation, career success |
| **Marriage Yoga** | D-9 | Spouse quality, marital happiness |
| **Children Yoga** | D-7, D-9 | Progeny confirmation |
| **Education Yoga** | D-24 | Academic success |

## Practical Application

### Reading Divisional Charts - Process

**For any life area question:**

1. **Start with D-1**
   - Analyze relevant house(s)
   - Check house lord position
   - Note karaka (significator) planet
   - Identify any yogas

2. **Move to D-9**
   - Confirms overall strength
   - Shows sustainability of results
   - Checks second half of life

3. **Examine specific divisional chart**
   - D-2 for wealth
   - D-7 for children
   - D-10 for career
   - D-24 for education
   - Etc.

4. **Synthesize findings**
   - Strong in D-1 + strong in specific chart = Excellent results
   - Strong in D-1 + weak in specific chart = Limited results in that area
   - Weak in D-1 + strong in specific chart = Delayed but eventual success

### Example: Career Analysis Using D-10

**Question:** "What kind of career will this person have?"

**Step 1 - D-1 Analysis:**
- 10th house sign: Capricorn (practical, authority-oriented)
- 10th lord Saturn in 5th house (creativity, intelligence applied to career)
- 10th lord aspect on 11th house (gains from career)

**Step 2 - D-9 Confirmation:**
- Saturn in D-9: Taurus (exalted) → Strong, reliable career in second half
- 10th house of D-9: Sagittarius → Philosophical, teaching, advisory work

**Step 3 - D-10 Detailed Analysis:**
- 10th house in D-10: Gemini (communication, versatility)
- Planets in 10th of D-10: Mercury (writing, speaking, trading)
- 10th lord of D-10 (Mercury) in D-10: Virgo (own sign, analytical)

**Step 4 - Synthesis:**
- **D-1:** Authoritative, structured career using intelligence
- **D-9:** Long-term success in advisory/teaching
- **D-10:** Communication, analysis, possibly writing or consulting
- **Conclusion:** Career as consultant, analyst, writer, or teacher in structured organization; success increases after age 35; multiple projects/income sources

### Common Mistakes in Divisional Chart Analysis

1. **Over-relying on divisional charts without strong D-1**
   - D-1 is foundation; weak D-1 limits divisional results

2. **Ignoring D-9 confirmation**
   - D-9 is crucial for sustained results

3. **Using divisional charts without accurate birth time**
   - Charts beyond D-12 need ±2-5 minute accuracy
   - D-60 needs ±1 minute accuracy (rarely available)

4. **Reading divisional charts in isolation**
   - Must correlate with D-1 and synthesis with overall horoscope

5. **Forgetting dasha activation**
   - Divisional strength manifests during appropriate dasha periods

## Advanced Concepts

### Karakamsa

**Definition:** The Navamsa occupied by the Atmakaraka (planet with highest degree in D-1)

**Significance:**
- Shows **soul's purpose** and deepest desires
- Indicates **profession** from soul level
- Reveals **spiritual evolution** path
- More refined than Lagna in showing inner nature

**Usage:**
- Count houses from Karakamsa in D-9
- Planets placed relative to Karakamsa show life themes
- Very important in Jaimini astrology

**Example:**
- Atmakaraka in D-1 is Sun at 25° Leo
- Sun at 25° Leo falls in Aries in D-9
- Karakamsa = Aries in D-9
- Read D-9 from Aries as lagna for soul-level insights

### Upapada Lagna

**Definition:** Navamsa position of the 12th lord from Arudha Lagna (AL)

**Calculation:**
1. Find Arudha Lagna in D-1
2. Count 12 houses from AL
3. Find lord of that house
4. Count same number of houses from that lord
5. Convert that sign's position to D-9 = Upapada

**Significance:**
- **Spouse** characteristics in deep detail
- **Marriage** quality and nature
- More specific than just 7th house of D-9

**Usage:** Advanced technique for marriage analysis

### Varnada

System of special lagnas in divisional charts for specific predictions:
- Different formulas for different divisional charts
- Used in advanced Jaimini and Parashari synthesis
- Beyond scope of basic divisional chart understanding

## Birth Time Rectification Using Divisional Charts

When birth time is uncertain (±15-30 minutes):

**Method:**
1. Use known life events (marriage, job, children birth)
2. Check which birth time places relevant planets favorably in divisional charts
3. For marriage event → Check D-9 7th lord dasha
4. For career event → Check D-10 10th lord dasha
5. For children → Check D-7 5th lord dasha
6. Adjust birth time until events align with divisional dasha periods

**Caution:** Requires expert-level understanding; should not be attempted without thorough training

## Conclusion

Divisional charts are the microscope of Vedic Astrology, providing precision and depth impossible with only the Rasi chart. Key principles:

- **D-1 (Rasi) is foundation** - Must be strong
- **D-9 (Navamsa) is king** - Confirms everything
- **Specific divisional charts** - Show specific life areas
- **Vargottama gives exceptional strength** - Same sign in D-1 and D-9
- **Yoga confirmation in D-9** - Necessary for sustained results
- **Accuracy matters** - Beyond D-12 requires very accurate birth time
- **Synthesis is key** - Integrate all charts for complete picture

Remember: "The Rasi chart is the tree trunk, Navamsa is the health of roots, and other divisional charts are the branches. All must be healthy for the tree to bear fruit."
