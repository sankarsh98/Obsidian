# Medical Astrology (Ayur Jyotish)

## Introduction

**Medical Astrology**, or **Ayur Jyotish**, correlates the planetary bodies and zodiac signs with the human anatomy, physiology, and potential for disease. It is deeply intertwined with **Ayurveda**, the traditional Indian system of medicine.

**Core Principle:**
> "Yat Pinde Tat Brahmande"
> (Whatever is in the body/microcosm is in the universe/macrocosm).

The birth chart reveals the physical constitution (Prakriti), mental constitution (Manas), and the timing of health crises (Rog).

## Planetary Significations (Karaktwas)

Each planet governs specific tissues (Dhatus) and physiological functions.

| Planet | Tissue (Dhatu) | System | Diseases |
|--------|----------------|--------|----------|
| **Sun** | Bones (Asthi) | Vitality, Heart, Eyes | Heart trouble, weak vision, low immunity, fever |
| **Moon** | Blood/Fluids (Rakta) | Mind, Fluids, Hormones | Mental illness, menstrual issues, cough/cold, anemia |
| **Mars** | Marrow (Majja) | Muscles, Blood (RBC) | Surgery, cuts, burns, hypertension, inflammation |
| **Mercury**| Skin (Tvak) | Nerves, Speech | Skin disease, speech defects, anxiety, nervous breakdown |
| **Jupiter**| Fat (Meda) | Liver, Pancreas, Ears | Diabetes, obesity, liver jaundice, ear infection |
| **Venus** | Semen (Shukra) | Reprod., Kidneys | Sexual dysfunction, urinary issues, diabetes, stones |
| **Saturn** | Nerves (Snayu) | Legs, Joints, Teeth | Chronic illness, paralysis, arthritis, depression |
| **Rahu** | -- | Unknown/Foreign | Cancer, poisoning, phobias, undiagnosed illness |
| **Ketu** | -- | Subtle/Genetic | Viral infection, genetic defects, mystery pain |

## The Three Doshas (Ayurvedic Constitution)

Planets correspond to the three humors (Doshas) that govern biological functions.

1.  **Vata (Air/Ether):** Movement, Nerves.
    *   *Planets:* **Saturn, Mercury, Rahu**.
    *   *Imbalance:* Pain, anxiety, paralysis, gas, dry skin.
2.  **Pitta (Fire/Water):** Digestion, Metabolism.
    *   *Planets:* **Sun, Mars, Ketu**.
    *   *Imbalance:* Inflammation, fever, acidity, ulcers, anger.
3.  **Kapha (Water/Earth):** Structure, Lubrication.
    *   *Planets:* **Moon, Venus, Jupiter**.
    *   *Imbalance:* Congestion, obesity, swelling, lethargy.

## Timing of Disease (Rog)

Health issues are usually activated when:
1.  **Dasha:** Running the Dasha of the **6th Lord** (Disease), **8th Lord** (Chronic/Surgery), or **12th Lord** (Hospitalization).
2.  **Maraka Dasha:** Dasha of 2nd or 7th Lord (Death inflicting) can cause severe health crises.
3.  **Transit:** Saturn or Rahu transiting over the Natal Moon or Ascendant.

## Medical Indicators in Divisional Charts

*   **D-1 (Rasi):** General physical strength.
*   **D-9 (Navamsa):** Inner strength and recovery potential.
*   **D-3 (Drekkana):** Specifically used for locating the seat of disease (Sarpa Drekkana indicates chronic illness).
*   **D-30 (Trimshamsa):** The specific chart for evils and diseases.
