# Transits (Gocharas)

## Introduction

**Transits**, known as **Gocharas** (गोचर - meaning "movement of cows" or "movement in the sky"), refer to the current ongoing movement of planets in the zodiac and their influence on an individual's [[Birth Chart (Kundali)]].

While the birth chart indicates the **potential** (Prarabdha Karma), transits indicate the **timing** of delivery. A fundamental rule in Vedic Astrology is:

> "The Dasha provides the direction, and the Transit provides the timing."
> (Dasha disha deti hai, Gochar samay batata hai)

Classical texts like **Phaladeepika** (Chapter 26) and **Brihat Parashara Hora Shastra** define the rules for analyzing transits.

## Primary Transit Principles

### 1. The Moon as Reference Point

In Vedic Astrology, transits are primarily analyzed from the **Natal Moon** (Janma Rashi), unlike Western astrology which focuses on the Ascendant or Sun.

**Why the Moon?**
- The Moon represents the mind (Manas) and perception.
- Transits affect how we *perceive* and *react* to events.
- The Moon is the fastest moving body, tracking the flow of time and experience.

**Secondary Reference:** Transits are also checked from the Ascendant (Lagna) for physical/material events, but the Moon is primary for psychological impact and general fortune.

### 2. Duration of Transits

The impact of a transit depends on how long the planet stays in a sign:

| Planet | Duration per Sign | Nature of Impact |
|--------|-------------------|------------------|
| **Moon** | 2.25 days | Moods, daily fluctuations |
| **Sun** | 1 month | Vitality, monthly focus, authority |
| **Mercury** | ~3 weeks | Communication, commerce, travel |
| **Venus** | ~3-4 weeks | Pleasure, relationships, comfort |
| **Mars** | ~1.5 months | Energy, conflicts, accidents |
| **Jupiter** | ~1 year | Growth, wisdom, major opportunities |
| **Rahu/Ketu** | ~1.5 years | Obsessions, detachments, karmic shifts |
| **Saturn** | ~2.5 years | Structure, delays, maturation, life-changing |

**Key Principle:** Slower-moving planets (Saturn, Jupiter, Rahu, Ketu) create **major life events**, while faster planets act as triggers.

## Analyzing Transits by House

Planets produce different results when transiting through different houses *from the Natal Moon*.

### Benefic vs. Malefic Transit Positions

Each planet has specific houses from the Moon where it yields beneficial results (Gochar), and others where it causes Vedha (obstruction) or negative results.

| Planet | Favorable Houses (from Moon) | Unfavorable Houses |
|--------|------------------------------|--------------------|
| **Sun** | 3, 6, 10, 11 | 1, 2, 4, 5, 7, 8, 9, 12 |
| **Moon** | 1, 3, 6, 7, 10, 11 | 2, 4, 5, 8, 9, 12 |
| **Mars** | 3, 6, 11 | 1, 2, 4, 5, 7, 8, 9, 10, 12 |
| **Mercury** | 2, 4, 6, 8, 10, 11 | 1, 3, 5, 7, 9, 12 |
| **Jupiter** | 2, 5, 7, 9, 11 | 1, 3, 4, 6, 8, 10, 12 |
| **Venus** | 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 8, 9, 11, 12 | 6, 7, 10 |
| **Saturn** | 3, 6, 11 | 1, 2, 4, 5, 7, 8, 9, 10, 12 |
| **Rahu** | 3, 6, 11 | All others |
| **Ketu** | 3, 6, 11 | All others |

### Detailed Transit Effects

#### 1. Saturn Transit (Shani Gochar) - The Great Judge

Saturn's transit is the most feared and respected. It brings karma to fruition.

- **3rd, 6th, 11th House (Good):** Victory over enemies, success, promotion, gains, happiness.
- **1st, 2nd, 12th House (Sade Sati):** Heavy mental pressure, separation, financial stress.
- **4th House (Ardha-Ashtama/Dhaiya):** Domestic unhappiness, property issues, mother's health.
- **8th House (Ashtama Shani):** Unexpected losses, health issues, delays, danger.
- **10th House:** Workplace conflict, loss of position, or heavy responsibility.

#### 2. Jupiter Transit (Guru Gochar) - The Great Benefactor

Jupiter's transit expands and protects.

- **2nd, 5th, 7th, 9th, 11th House (Good):** Wealth, children, marriage, fortune, spiritual growth.
- **1st House (Janma Guru):** Change of place, heavy expenses, loss of wealth (though spiritual growth).
- **3rd House:** Job changes, obstacles, loss of position.
- **4th House:** Domestic trouble, distress to mother/relatives.
- **6th House:** Health issues, trouble from enemies, debts.
- **8th House:** Fatigue, misfortune, travel.
- **10th House:** Career changes, loss of status, humble work.
- **12th House:** Grief, separation, expenses.

#### 3. Rahu/Ketu Transits (Nodal Axis)

- **Rahu in 3, 6, 11:** Tremendous material success, victory, sudden gains.
- **Rahu in 1, 5, 9:** Confusion, mistakes in judgment, unconventional choices.
- **Ketu in 3, 6, 11:** Spiritual victory, end of conflicts.
- **Ketu in 1, 2, 8:** Health issues, accidents, detachment, family trouble.

## Major Transit Phenomena

### 1. Sade Sati (The 7.5 Years of Saturn)

**Definition:** When Saturn transits the 12th, 1st, and 2nd houses from the Natal Moon.
- Duration: ~2.5 years × 3 signs = 7.5 years.
- Occurs: Every ~29.5 years.

**Phases:**
1.  **First Phase (12th from Moon):** Financial strain, expenses, aimless travel, eye trouble, feeling of isolation. Focus is on loss and letting go.
2.  **Second Phase (1st from Moon - Janma Shani):** Peak pressure. Health issues, mental anxiety, reputation at risk, challenges to self-identity.
3.  **Third Phase (2nd from Moon):** Financial recovery but slow accumulation, family disputes, harsh speech.

**Result:** It strips away false security and ego, forcing maturity. For those with strong charts/dasha, it can bring massive success through hard work (e.g., becoming Prime Minister).

### 2. Dhaiya (Small Panoti)

- **4th House Transit (Kantaka Shani):** "Thorn in the leg." Career struggles, domestic unrest, chest/heart issues.
- **8th House Transit (Ashtama Shani):** Most difficult 2.5 years. Sudden endings, chronic illness, humiliation, danger to life.

### 3. Double Transit Theory

A key predictive technique for timing events like marriage or childbirth. An event is likely to manifest when **both** Jupiter and Saturn influence a house:

1.  **Saturn** (the Karmic deliveryman) aspects a house, indicating the karma is "ready."
2.  **Jupiter** (the Divine blessing) aspects the same house, indicating the "blessing/permission" is granted.

**Example (Marriage):**
If Saturn transits Aquarius (aspecting Aries) and Jupiter transits Sagittarius (aspecting Aries), the 7th house (Aries) is activated for marriage.

## Vedha (Obstruction)

A transit that is normally good can be **blocked** (Vedha) if another planet occupies a specific relative position.

*Example:* Sun in 11th is good. But if a planet is in the 5th house from Moon at the same time, the good result of the Sun is obstructed.

*(Table of Vedha points is complex and used in advanced calculation).*

## Moorti Nirnaya (Determining the Form)

When a major planet (Saturn, Jupiter, Rahu, Ketu) changes signs, its effect is modified by the **Moorti** (Form) determined by the Moon's position at the exact moment of ingress.

1.  **Swarna (Gold) Moorti:** Moon in 1st, 6th, 11th from Natal Moon.
    *   *Result:* Extremely auspicious, heavy gains.
2.  **Rajat (Silver) Moorti:** Moon in 2nd, 5th, 9th.
    *   *Result:* Very good, status, fame.
3.  **Tamra (Copper) Moorti:** Moon in 3rd, 7th, 10th.
    *   *Result:* Average, mixed results.
4.  **Loha (Iron) Moorti:** Moon in 4th, 8th, 12th.
    *   *Result:* Inauspicious, heavy struggles.

## Nakshatra Transits (Tara Bala)

Transits are also analyzed by the [[Nakshatras]] the planet is passing through relative to the **Janma Nakshatra** (Birth Star).

The sequence of 9 Taras (Stars) repeats:
1.  **Janma (Birth):** Danger to body/mind.
2.  **Sampat (Wealth):** Financial gains.
3.  **Vipat (Danger):** Accidents, losses.
4.  **Kshema (Well-being):** Comfort, safety.
5.  **Pratyak (Obstacle):** Blocks, enemies.
6.  **Sadhana (Achievement):** Success, realization.
7.  **Naidhana (Death/Destruction):** Severe danger.
8.  **Mitra (Friend):** Happiness, support.
9.  **Parama Mitra (Great Friend):** Great help.

*Rule:* A malefic planet transiting a Vipat (3), Pratyak (5), or Naidhana (7) star causes severe problems.

## Synthesis: Dasha and Transit

**The Golden Rule of Prediction:**

1.  **Check Dasha:** Is the current Mahadasha/Antardasha lord promising the event? (e.g., 7th lord for marriage).
2.  **Check Transit:** Is Jupiter/Saturn activating the house? Is the Dasha lord strong in transit?
3.  **Check Natal Strength:** Does the chart promise the event at all?

**Example Scenario:**
- **Natal:** 10th lord is strong (Career success promised).
- **Dasha:** Running Dasha of 10th lord (Time is ripe).
- **Transit:** Saturn transiting 10th house (Work pressure) + Jupiter transiting 2nd aspecting 10th (Salary hike).
- **Result:** Promotion with heavy responsibilities.
