## 🎓 Improvement Recommendations

### Priority 1: Fix the Englund Gambit Response
```
After 1. d4 e5 2. dxe5 Nc6 3. Nf3 Qe7:
→ Play 4. Nc3! (not Bf4)
→ 4... Qb4 5. Bd2 Qxb2 6. Nd5! threatens Nc7+
→ This is a well-known refutation — memorize it!
```

### Priority 2: Caro-Kann Advance — Anti-Tal Prep
```
After 1. e4 c6 2. d4 d5 3. e5 Bf5:
→ vs 4. h4 (Tal Variation): play 4...h5! to prevent g4
→ vs 4. Nf3 e6 5.Be2: play 5...c5 immediately to challenge center
→ Study games of Karpov in the Advance Caro-Kann
```

### Priority 3: Time Management
- Use the first 3 minutes of a 10+0 game for the opening (move quickly)
- Spend maximum time on critical moves (moves 15-25)
- Never spend more than 2 minutes on a single move after move 25

### Priority 4: Endgame Training
- Focus on **Rook endgames** (Lucena, Philidor positions)
- Practice **King activity** in endgames
