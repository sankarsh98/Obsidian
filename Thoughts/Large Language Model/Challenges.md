# Challenges in LLMs

While powerful, LLMs face significant limitations and risks.

## Core Issues

-   **[[Hallucination]]**: The tendency to confidently state falsehoods.
-   **[[Bias in LLMs]]**: Reflecting and amplifying societal prejudices.
-   **Complexity**: The "Black Box" nature makes them hard to interpret.

## Other Challenges
-   **Cost**: High inference and training costs.
-   **Latency**: Generating text token-by-token is slow.
-   **Copyright**: Legal issues regarding training data.
