# LLM Techniques

Methods and strategies to get the most out of LLMs, from prompting to external data integration.

## Prompting
-   **[[Prompt Engineering]]**: The art of guiding the model.
-   **Chain of Thought**: Asking the model to "think step by step".

## Augmentation
-   **[[RAG|Retrieval Augmented Generation]]**: Connecting the model to your own data.
-   **Function Calling**: Allowing the model to execute code or call APIs.
